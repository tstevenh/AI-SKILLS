## Notes on merged cross-domain skills

These skills intentionally span multiple categories, but they are assigned one primary home for navigation:

- `frontend` sits in Engineering, but also covers design direction and UI review.
- `security` sits in Security, but also includes secops and agent-defense material.
- `testing` sits in Security/QA, but also includes browser automation and design QA.
- `growth-kit` sits in Product/Strategy, but routes into growth, content, and market work.
