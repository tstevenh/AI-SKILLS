# SaaS Growth Playbook

> The definitive guide to building, growing, and scaling SaaS businesses. 100,000+ words of actionable strategies, frameworks, benchmarks, and templates.

## Overview

This skill provides comprehensive SaaS growth knowledge covering:

1. **Fundamentals** - Business models, metrics, unit economics
2. **Product-Led Growth** - Free trials, freemium, onboarding, virality
3. **Pricing & Packaging** - Strategy, models, psychology, changes
4. **Acquisition Channels** - Content, paid, outbound, partnerships
5. **Conversion Optimization** - Website, demo, email, in-app
6. **Retention & Churn** - Prevention, recovery, customer success
7. **Expansion Revenue** - Upsells, cross-sells, NRR
8. **Operations & Scaling** - Team structure, tech stack, financial planning
9. **Case Studies** - Slack, Notion, Calendly, Loom, and more

## When to Use This Skill

- Planning SaaS pricing strategy
- Optimizing trial-to-paid conversion
- Reducing churn
- Designing onboarding flows
- Building acquisition channels
- Scaling operations
- Analyzing SaaS metrics

## Quick Reference

### Key Metrics Benchmarks

| Metric | Good | Great | Elite |
|--------|------|-------|-------|
| Monthly Churn | <5% | <3% | <1% |
| LTV:CAC Ratio | 3:1 | 4:1 | 5:1+ |
| Net Revenue Retention | >100% | >110% | >130% |
| Trial Conversion | >15% | >25% | >40% |
| CAC Payback | <18mo | <12mo | <6mo |

### Growth Levers by Stage

**$0-100K ARR (Finding PMF)**
- Focus: Product, early users, learning
- Primary channel: Founder sales, communities

**$100K-$1M ARR (Initial Traction)**
- Focus: Repeatable acquisition, basic retention
- Primary channel: Content, PLG optimization

**$1M-$10M ARR (Scaling)**
- Focus: Channel diversification, team building
- Primary channel: Paid, partnerships, sales

**$10M+ ARR (Optimization)**
- Focus: Efficiency, expansion revenue, enterprise
- Primary channel: All channels optimized
