# Upgrade Email Templates

---

## Approaching Limit

```
Subject: You're almost at your [limit type] limit

Hi [First Name],

Great news—you're getting a lot of value from [Product]! 
You've used [X] of your [Y] [limit type] this month.

When you hit your limit, you can:
• Wait until next month (resets [Date])
• Upgrade for unlimited [limit type]

Ready for more?
[Button: Upgrade to [Plan] →]

[Your Name]
```

[Your Name]

## Annual Upgrade (Monthly → Annual)

```

Hi [First Name],

You've been with us for [X months]—awesome!

Quick math:
• You pay: $[Monthly] × 12 = $[Annual at monthly rate]
• Annual plan: $[Annual price]
• You save: $[Savings]

Same features, less cost. Easy switch:
[Button: Switch to Annual →]

[Your Name]
```

## Soft Upsell (Content-Led)

```
Subject: [Resource] to help you [achieve goal]

Hi [First Name],

I put together [resource type] on [topic]—thought you 
might find it useful.

[Link to resource]

By the way, if you're looking to [related goal], our 
[Plan] customers typically see [result]. Happy to chat 
about it if you're curious.

[Your Name]
```
