# Local Outreach

## The Local Outreach Difference

Local link building outreach differs from general outreach in important ways:

**Relationship matters more:**
In a local community, you may meet these people. Reputation carries.

**Easier to personalize:**
Local knowledge provides natural conversation starters.

**Multiple touchpoints:**
Local outreach often involves in-person and digital connections.

**Long-term perspective:**
Local relationships compound over years, not campaigns.

## Templates for Local Outreach

**Subject:** Membership inquiry - [Your Business Name]

```

[Phone]
```

## Building Local Relationships

### The Long-Term Approach

**Phase 1: Awareness**
- Attend local events
- Join local organizations
- Comment on local issues
- Be visible in community

**Phase 2: Connection**
- Introduce yourself
- Find common ground
- Offer help without agenda
- Build genuine relationships

**Phase 3: Collaboration**
- Propose specific partnerships
- Work together on projects
- Create mutual value
- Formalize link opportunities

### Local Networking for Links

**Events to attend:**
- Chamber meetings
- Business association events
- Industry local meetups
- Community gatherings
- Local conferences

**How to network for links:**
- Don't pitch at events
- Build relationships first
- Follow up after meeting
- Find genuine connection points
- Let opportunities emerge naturally

## Event-Based Link Building

### Creating Your Own Events

**Types of events:**
- Educational workshops
- Networking events
- Community service activities
- Grand openings/anniversaries
- Customer appreciation events

**Link opportunities:**
- Event coverage by local media
- Partner/sponsor mentions
- Community calendar listings
- Social media coverage
- Post-event write-ups

### Leveraging Others' Events

**Participation opportunities:**
- Speaking engagements
- Panel participation
- Event sponsorship
- Vendor/exhibitor presence
- Volunteer involvement

**Post-event outreach:**
- Thank sponsors and organizers
- Connect with people you met
- Share event coverage
- Propose future collaboration

## Measuring Local Link Building

### Metrics to Track

**Link metrics:**
- Local referring domains
- Links from local sources
- .edu and .gov links
- Directory citations

**Local SEO metrics:**
- Local Pack rankings
- Local organic rankings
- Google Business Profile visibility
- Local search impressions

**Relationship metrics:**
- Local partnerships formed
- Community involvement level
- Local reputation/recognition

### ROI for Local Links

**Direct value:**
- Local organic traffic
- Local lead generation
- Local sales attribution

**Indirect value:**
- Brand recognition locally
- Referral business
- Community goodwill
- Long-term relationship value

## Summary

Local outreach requires:

1. **Relationship focus** - Build connections before asking
2. **Personalization** - Use local knowledge
3. **Multiple channels** - Digital and in-person
4. **Long-term thinking** - Relationships compound
5. **Community involvement** - Real participation, not just link seeking

Local links come from being an active, valuable member of your community—not just from email campaigns.
