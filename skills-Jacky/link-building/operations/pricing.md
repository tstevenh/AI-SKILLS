# Pricing & Packaging

## Pricing Models

### Model 1: Per-Link Pricing

**How it works:**
Charge a fixed fee for each link delivered.

**Price structure:**

| DR Range | Standard Price | Premium Price |
|----------|----------------|---------------|
| 20-29 | $75-100 | $125-150 |
| 30-39 | $125-175 | $200-250 |
| 40-49 | $175-275 | $300-400 |
| 50-59 | $275-400 | $450-600 |
| 60-69 | $400-600 | $650-900 |
| 70+ | $600-1,000 | $1,000-1,500+ |

**Pros:**
- Simple to understand
- Clear deliverables
- Easy to compare
- Scales with output

**Cons:**
- Commoditizes service
- Price pressure from competitors
- Doesn't capture strategy value
- Can incentivize quantity over quality

**Best for:**
- Transactional clients
- One-time projects
- Budget-conscious clients
- Clear scope requirements

### Model 2: Monthly Retainer

**How it works:**
Monthly fee for ongoing link building with guaranteed minimums.

**Typical packages:**

**Starter: $1,500-2,500/month**
- 5-8 links
- DR 30+ average
- Monthly reporting

**Growth: $3,000-5,000/month**
- 10-15 links
- DR 35+ average
- Bi-weekly reporting
- Strategy input

**Scale: $5,000-10,000/month**
- 20-35 links
- DR 40+ average
- Weekly reporting
- Strategy consultation
- Priority support

**Enterprise: $10,000+/month**
- 40+ links
- Custom DR requirements
- Dedicated team
- Strategic partnership
- Custom reporting

**Pros:**
- Predictable revenue
- Relationship building
- Comprehensive service
- Strategic involvement

**Cons:**
- Requires delivery consistency
- Higher client expectations
- More account management
- Harder to scale up

**Best for:**
- Ongoing partnerships
- Clients wanting full service
- Agencies with capacity
- Relationship-focused selling

### Model 3: Performance/Hybrid

**How it works:**
Base retainer plus performance bonuses.

**Structure example:**
- Base: $2,000/month for 5 guaranteed links
- Performance: $150/link for additional links
- Bonus: $500 for ranking improvements

**Pros:**
- Aligned incentives
- Upside potential
- Client feels protected
- Rewards outperformance

**Cons:**
- Complex to track
- Variable revenue
- Harder to staff
- Requires clear metrics

**Best for:**
- Performance-oriented clients
- Results-confident agencies
- Long-term relationships
- Sophisticated buyers

### Model 4: Project-Based

**How it works:**
Fixed fee for defined project scope.

**Example projects:**
- Link audit: $2,000-5,000
- 20-link campaign: $4,000-8,000
- Outreach campaign: $3,000-6,000
- Strategy development: $1,500-3,000

**Pros:**
- Clear scope
- Defined timeline
- Easier to sell
- Good for new clients

**Cons:**
- Scope creep risk
- No recurring revenue
- Start-stop efficiency loss
- Re-selling required

**Best for:**
- New client relationships
- Specific needs
- Budget-limited clients
- Proof of concept

## Package Design

### What to Include

**Core deliverables:**
- Link quantity
- Quality thresholds (DR, traffic)
- Content (if applicable)
- Reporting

**Value-adds:**
- Strategy consultation
- Anchor text planning
- Competitor analysis
- Link profile audit
- Dedicated account manager

### Package Examples

**Example 1: Startup Package ($2,000/month)**

```
STARTUP LINK BUILDING - $2,000/month

Links: 8 per month
Quality: DR 30+, 1,000+ traffic
Content: Included
Relevance: Topically relevant to your niche

Reporting: Monthly report with:
• Links delivered
• Metrics summary
• Next month plan

Contract: 3-month minimum
Setup: $500 one-time (includes audit)
```

**Example 2: Growth Package ($4,500/month)**

```
GROWTH LINK BUILDING - $4,500/month

Links: 15 per month
Quality: DR 35+, 5,000+ traffic average
Content: Premium content included
Relevance: High topical relevance

Includes:
• Bi-weekly progress updates
• Anchor text strategy
• Quarterly strategy calls
• Dedicated account manager

Reporting: Weekly dashboard + monthly review

Contract: 6-month minimum
Setup: Waived with annual commitment
```

**Example 3: Enterprise Package (Custom)**

```

• Weekly status meetings
• Strategic partnership

• Priority support

• Custom reporting

Contact for custom pricing based on requirements.
```

## ROI Justification

### Calculating Link Value

**For clients:**
"What is ranking worth to you?"

**Formula:**
Monthly search volume × CTR × Conversion rate × Customer value = Monthly value

**Example:**
- Keyword: 10,000 searches/month
- Position 3 CTR: 10%
- Traffic: 1,000 visitors/month
- Conversion rate: 3%
- Conversions: 30/month
- Customer value: $500
- Monthly value: $15,000

**Required links to rank:** ~20 at DR 40+
**Campaign cost:** ~$8,000
**Payback:** <1 month

### Value Messaging

**For different audiences:**

**For ROI-focused clients:**
"Our average client sees 3x ROI within 6 months based on ranking improvements and traffic gains."

**For brand-focused clients:**
"These placements build long-term authority that compounds over years."

**For competitive clients:**
"Your competitors are building X links per month. This package puts you on par."

## Pricing Psychology

### Anchoring

Present premium option first:
1. Enterprise ($15,000) - Anchors high
2. Growth ($4,500) - Seems reasonable
3. Starter ($2,000) - Entry point

### Three-Tier Strategy

Always offer three options:
- Budget option (basic)
- Recommended option (most value)
- Premium option (all features)

Most clients choose middle option.

### Annual Discounts

Offer 10-15% for annual commitments:
- Improves cash flow
- Reduces churn
- Locks in relationship

## Contract Terms

### Standard Terms

**Minimum commitment:** 3-6 months
- Link building takes time
- Allows strategy to work
- Provides delivery runway

**Payment terms:**
- Monthly in advance
- Net 15-30 for enterprise
- Setup fee on signing

**Scope definitions:**
- Link quality thresholds
- Content specifications
- Reporting schedule
- Communication expectations

### What to Protect

**In contracts:**
- Quality definitions (DR, traffic minimums)
- Delivery timelines
- Revision limits for content
- Change request process
- Termination terms

## Summary

Pricing success requires:

1. **Choose appropriate model** - Retainer, per-link, or hybrid
2. **Design clear packages** - Understandable deliverables
3. **Justify value** - ROI calculations
4. **Use psychology** - Anchoring, three tiers
5. **Protect with contracts** - Clear terms

Price based on value delivered, not just cost to produce.
