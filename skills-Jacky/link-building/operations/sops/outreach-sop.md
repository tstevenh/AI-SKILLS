# Outreach SOP

## Purpose

This SOP defines the standard process for conducting link building outreach. Following this process ensures consistent quality, professionalism, and results.

## Scope

Applies to: All outreach specialists and link builders conducting email outreach.

## Process Overview

```
1. Review Prospects → 2. Research & Personalize → 3. Send Outreach → 4. Follow Up → 5. Handle Responses → 6. Close & Document
```

## Step 1: Review Prospects

### Daily Preparation

1. Review prospects in "Outreach Queue" status
2. Check that all required information is present:
   - Domain and URL
   - Contact information
   - Qualification notes
3. Prioritize by:
   - Campaign urgency
   - Opportunity quality (DR, relevance)
   - Time since added (don't let prospects age)

### Daily Volume Targets

| Experience Level | Emails/Day | Quality Check |
|------------------|------------|---------------|
| Training | 10-15 | All reviewed |
| Developing | 15-25 | Spot checks |
| Proficient | 25-40 | Self-managed |
| Expert | 40-60 | Self-managed |

## Step 2: Research & Personalize

### Required Research

Before sending any outreach:

**For guest post outreach:**
- [ ] Read 1-2 recent articles on the site
- [ ] Note something specific to reference
- [ ] Check submission guidelines if available
- [ ] Prepare topic ideas relevant to their audience

**For niche edit outreach:**
- [ ] Read the target article
- [ ] Identify specific section for link placement
- [ ] Note how your resource fits
- [ ] Find specific reference for personalization

### Personalization Requirements

**Minimum personalization:**
- Recipient's name (or "Hi there" if unknown)
- Site name
- Specific page/article reference
- Relevant pitch for their audience

**Better personalization:**
- Reference to specific content/quote
- Understanding of their audience
- Tailored topic suggestion
- Evidence you know their site

### Template Selection

Choose appropriate template based on:
- Opportunity type (guest post, niche edit, resource)
- Site tier (premium, standard, budget)
- Previous relationship (cold, warm)

**Customize template with:**
- Personalization elements
- Specific pitch/topic
- Correct contact info
- Campaign-specific details

## Step 3: Send Outreach

### Email Quality Checklist

Before sending each email:

- [ ] Recipient name correct
- [ ] Site name correct
- [ ] URL references correct
- [ ] Personalization included
- [ ] Grammar and spelling checked
- [ ] Links working
- [ ] Signature complete
- [ ] Subject line appropriate

### Sending Best Practices

**Timing:**
- Send Tuesday-Thursday when possible
- Morning hours (9-11 AM recipient's time zone)
- Avoid Monday mornings and Friday afternoons

**Volume:**
- Spread sends throughout day
- Don't send more than 50 from one account
- Monitor deliverability

**Platform:**
- Use approved outreach tool
- Track all sends in CRM
- Enable tracking (opens, clicks)

### After Sending

- Update status to "Contacted"
- Add "Date Contacted" field
- Schedule follow-up (5-7 days)

## Step 4: Follow Up

### Follow-Up Schedule

| Follow-Up | Timing | Action |
|-----------|--------|--------|
| 1st | Day 5-7 | Short reminder |
| 2nd | Day 12-14 | Final follow-up |
| Close | Day 21+ | Move to "No Response" |

### Follow-Up Templates

**Follow-Up 1:**
```
Hi [Name],

Just floating this back up in case it got buried. Still interested in [brief reminder of pitch].

Let me know if you have any questions.

Best,
[Name]
```

**Follow-Up 2:**
```
Hi [Name],

Last follow-up on this - I know you're busy.

If [opportunity] isn't a fit right now, no problem. But if there's interest, I'm still happy to discuss.

Thanks,
[Name]
```

### Follow-Up Execution

- Use outreach tool automation when available
- Manually send if tool lacks follow-up features
- Track all follow-ups in CRM
- Stop after 2nd follow-up (don't spam)

## Step 5: Handle Responses

### Response Categories

**Positive:**
- "Yes, send more info"
- "We'd love to publish"
- "Here are our rates"
- "Let's move forward"

**Neutral:**
- "Maybe, check back later"
- "We're reviewing"
- "Not right now but keep in touch"
- "Need more information"

**Negative:**
- "No, not interested"
- "We don't accept guest posts"
- "Not a fit"
- "Stop emailing me"

### Response Handling

**For positive responses:**
1. Respond within 24 hours
2. Provide requested information
3. Clarify next steps
4. Move to negotiation/content phase
5. Update status to "In Progress"

**For neutral responses:**
1. Thank them
2. Provide any requested info
3. Schedule follow-up for suggested timeframe
4. Keep relationship warm
5. Update notes with context

**For negative responses:**
1. Thank them professionally
2. Ask for referrals (optional)
3. Don't argue or push
4. Update status to "Rejected"
5. Note reason for future reference

### Negotiation (When Applicable)

**If they quote a price:**
- Evaluate against campaign budget
- Counter if above budget: "Could you do $X?"
- Accept if within range
- Walk away professionally if too high

**If they ask your budget:**
- State your range: "We typically work at $X-Y"
- Ask their rate: "What do you usually charge?"

**Document all negotiations in CRM notes.**

## Step 6: Close & Document

### For Accepted Opportunities

1. Confirm all details:
   - Price (if paid)
   - Timeline
   - Content requirements
   - Link specifications (URL, anchor)
2. Document agreement in CRM
3. Hand off to content/next step
4. Update status to "Accepted"

### For Completed Links

1. Verify link is live (see QA SOP)
2. Document placement details
3. Update status to "Live"
4. Note in campaign reporting

### For Closed-Lost

1. Update status appropriately:
   - "Rejected" - They declined
   - "No Response" - No response after follow-ups
   - "Disqualified" - Discovered quality issue
2. Note reason in CRM
3. Consider for future (if just timing)

## Quality Standards

### Email Quality Requirements

All outreach must:
- Be grammatically correct
- Be properly personalized
- Have accurate information
- Follow template guidelines
- Be professional in tone

### Response Time Standards

- Initial responses: Within 24 hours
- Negotiations: Within 24-48 hours
- Information requests: Within 24 hours

### Professionalism

- Never be rude or aggressive
- Don't spam (max 2 follow-ups)
- Respect "no" responses
- Maintain company reputation

## Metrics & KPIs

### Activity Metrics

- Outreach emails sent/day
- Follow-ups sent/day
- Responses handled/day

### Performance Metrics

- Response rate (target: 10-15%+)
- Positive response rate (target: 5-10%+)
- Link conversion rate (target: 3-8%+)
- Average response time

### Quality Metrics

- Email quality score (spot-checked)
- Professional conduct (no complaints)
- CRM accuracy

## Common Mistakes

### Mistake: Generic templates

**Problem:** No personalization
**Solution:** Research before sending

### Mistake: Wrong details

**Problem:** Wrong name, site, or link
**Solution:** Double-check before sending

### Mistake: Over-following-up

**Problem:** More than 2 follow-ups
**Solution:** Respect their time, move on

### Mistake: Poor response handling

**Problem:** Slow or unprofessional responses
**Solution:** Respond quickly and professionally

### Mistake: Not documenting

**Problem:** Lost information, duplicate outreach
**Solution:** Update CRM for every interaction
