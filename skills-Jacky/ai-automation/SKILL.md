## Key Principles

### 1. Start with ROI
Never automate for automation's sake. Calculate:
- Hours saved per week
- Cost of automation (tools + setup)
- Payback period

### 2. Human-in-the-Loop
For anything customer-facing or high-stakes:
- AI generates draft
- Human reviews/approves
- Gradual trust building

### 3. Progressive Automation
1. **Manual** → Document the process
2. **Assisted** → AI helps, human decides
3. **Supervised** → AI does, human approves
4. **Autonomous** → AI handles end-to-end

### 4. Build for Failure
- Always have fallbacks
- Log everything
- Alert on anomalies
- Easy manual override
