# Community Guidelines Template

## Overview

Clear community guidelines set expectations, protect culture, and give moderators authority to act. This template provides a comprehensive framework you can adapt for your community.

---

## Full Community Guidelines Template

```
# [COMMUNITY NAME] COMMUNITY GUIDELINES

Last updated: [DATE]

Welcome to [Community Name]! These guidelines exist to ensure 
everyone has a positive, productive experience.

---

## OUR MISSION

[Community Name] exists to [MISSION STATEMENT].

We believe [CORE BELIEF].

We're committed to creating a space where [IDEAL ENVIRONMENT].

---

## OUR VALUES

These values guide everything we do:

**[VALUE 1: e.g., RESPECT]**
We treat every member with dignity, regardless of background, 
experience level, or perspective. We can disagree without 
being disagreeable.

**[VALUE 2: e.g., GENEROSITY]**
We give before we take. Sharing knowledge, connections, and 
support makes us all stronger.

**[VALUE 3: e.g., GROWTH]**
We're all here to improve. We embrace learning, celebrate 
progress, and support each other's journeys.

**[VALUE 4: e.g., AUTHENTICITY]**
We show up as ourselves. We're honest about our struggles 
and genuine in our interactions.

---

## EXPECTED BEHAVIORS

We expect all members to:

✅ **Be Respectful**
Treat others the way you'd want to be treated. Assume good 
intent. Be constructive, not critical.

✅ **Be Helpful**
Share your knowledge. Answer questions. Celebrate others' wins.
A rising tide lifts all boats.

✅ **Be On-Topic**
Keep discussions relevant to the community focus. Use 
appropriate channels for different topics.

✅ **Be Professional**
This is a professional community. Keep language and content 
appropriate for a work environment.

✅ **Be Accountable**
Own your mistakes. If you mess up, apologize and do better. 
We're all learning.

✅ **Be Present**
Participate. Lurking is okay sometimes, but the community 
thrives when members engage.

---

## PROHIBITED BEHAVIORS

The following are not tolerated:

❌ **Harassment or Discrimination**
No bullying, intimidation, or discrimination based on race, 
gender, sexuality, religion, disability, or any other characteristic.

❌ **Hate Speech**
No slurs, derogatory language, or content promoting hate 
against any group.

❌ **Spam or Excessive Self-Promotion**
Don't flood the community with promotional content. Follow 
our self-promotion guidelines below.

❌ **Sharing Private Information**
Don't share other members' personal information (doxxing) 
or confidential community content.

❌ **Illegal Content**
Nothing illegal. This includes pirated content, drugs, fraud, 
or anything that violates the law.

❌ **Trolling or Intentional Disruption**
Don't post to provoke, derail discussions, or create drama.

❌ **Impersonation**
Don't pretend to be someone else, including other members, 
moderators, or public figures.

❌ **NSFW Content**
No explicit sexual content, graphic violence, or other 
not-safe-for-work material.

---

## SELF-PROMOTION POLICY

We welcome sharing your work with important guidelines:

**DO:**
✓ Share in designated promotional channels
✓ Provide context and value with your promotion
✓ Be transparent about what you're promoting
✓ Engage genuinely beyond just promoting
✓ Respond to comments on your promotional posts

**DON'T:**
✗ Spam multiple channels with promotions
✗ Send unsolicited promotional DMs
✗ Make every post about your product/service
✗ Use misleading tactics
✗ Promote competitors' products without permission

**Affiliate Links:**
Ask a moderator before posting affiliate links. Generally, 
they're okay if clearly disclosed and genuinely helpful.

**Where to Promote:**
[DESIGNATED PROMOTIONAL CHANNEL/AREA]

---

## CONFLICT RESOLUTION

Disagreements happen. Here's how we handle them:

**Step 1: Assume Good Intent**
Before reacting, consider that the other person may have 
miscommunicated or you may have misunderstood.

**Step 2: Address Directly (If Comfortable)**
If appropriate, reach out privately to resolve the issue.
Keep it respectful and focused on the behavior, not the person.

**Step 3: Involve Moderators**
If you can't resolve it, contact a moderator. We'll help 
mediate or take action as needed.

**What NOT to Do:**
- Don't escalate publicly
- Don't recruit others to "take sides"
- Don't retaliate
- Don't let it fester

---

## CONSEQUENCES

We use a progressive approach to enforcement:

**Level 1: Warning**
First-time minor violations receive a private warning 
explaining the issue and expected change.

**Level 2: Temporary Restriction**
Repeated minor violations or moderate violations may result 
in temporary muting, loss of privileges, or short suspension.

**Level 3: Extended Suspension**
Continued violations or serious misconduct results in 
longer suspension and formal review.

**Level 4: Removal**
Severe violations (harassment, hate speech, illegal activity) 
or repeated violations after warnings result in permanent removal.

**Appeals:**
If you believe a decision was made in error, contact 
[EMAIL] with your appeal. Include:
- What happened from your perspective
- Why you believe the decision was incorrect
- What you'll do differently

We review all appeals within [X] business days.

---

## REPORTING ISSUES

If you see something that violates these guidelines:

**How to Report:**
1. Use the report/flag feature on the post
2. DM a moderator: @[MODERATOR NAMES]
3. Email: [MODERATION EMAIL]

**What to Include:**
- What happened
- Where (link if possible)
- Who was involved
- Any screenshots

**What Happens Next:**
- We review within [X] hours
- We take appropriate action
- We follow up with you if needed

**Confidentiality:**
Reports are confidential. We don't share who reported what.

---

## PRIVACY & DATA

**Your Information:**
We collect and store information as outlined in our 
Privacy Policy: [LINK]

**What We Don't Share:**
- Your personal information
- Your activity details
- Private messages

**What May Be Shared:**
- Aggregate, anonymized data
- Public posts (they're public)

**Member Privacy:**
Respect others' privacy. Don't share:
- Private conversations without consent
- Personal information about others
- Community-only content externally

---

## INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

**Your Content:**
You own what you create. By posting, you grant [Community Name] 
a license to display it within the community.

**Others' Content:**
Don't post copyrighted material without permission. Give 
credit when sharing others' ideas.

**Community Resources:**
Materials created for the community are for member use only. 
Don't redistribute without permission.

---

## CHANGES TO GUIDELINES

We may update these guidelines as the community evolves.

**When We Update:**
- Major changes announced in [ANNOUNCEMENTS]
- Minor clarifications may happen without announcement

**Your Responsibility:**
Review guidelines periodically. Continued participation 
means acceptance of current guidelines.

---

## AGREEMENT

By participating in [Community Name], you agree to follow 
these guidelines.

If you have questions about anything here, contact:
- Moderators: @[NAMES] or [CHANNEL]
- Admin: [EMAIL]

Thank you for being part of our community!

---

**Questions?** Contact us at [EMAIL] or post in [HELP CHANNEL].
```

## Short-Form Guidelines Template

For communities that need something simpler:

```
# [COMMUNITY NAME] COMMUNITY RULES

1. **Be Respectful** - Treat everyone with kindness
2. **Be Helpful** - Share knowledge, support others
3. **Stay On Topic** - Keep discussions relevant
4. **No Spam** - Promote only in designated areas
5. **No Harassment** - Zero tolerance for bullying or discrimination
6. **Keep It Legal** - Nothing illegal or harmful

**Self-Promotion:** Only in #[promotion-channel]

**Issues?** DM @[moderator] or flag the post

**Violations:** Warning → Mute → Removal

By participating, you agree to these rules.
```

## Implementation Notes

### Publishing Guidelines

- Pin in community
- Include in welcome sequence
- Reference in onboarding
- Review with moderators
- Update annually

### Enforcement Consistency

- Document all actions
- Discuss edge cases with team
- Apply rules equally
- Review periodically
