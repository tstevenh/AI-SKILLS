# Community Engagement Checklist

## Overview

Use this checklist to audit your community engagement and identify opportunities for improvement. Complete it monthly or quarterly to ensure your community stays healthy and engaging.

---

## Daily Engagement Audit

### Founder/Team Presence
- [ ] Founder or team member active daily
- [ ] Responses to posts within 24 hours
- [ ] New member welcomes happening
- [ ] Questions being answered
- [ ] Positive engagement modeled

### Content Flow
- [ ] Daily discussion prompt posted
- [ ] Educational content shared (if scheduled)
- [ ] Member content engaged with
- [ ] Wins and celebrations highlighted
- [ ] Questions redirected to resources when appropriate

### Community Health Signals
- [ ] New posts appearing regularly
- [ ] Comments on posts (not just founder)
- [ ] Member-to-member interactions happening
- [ ] No toxic behavior or spam
- [ ] Positive sentiment overall

---

## Weekly Engagement Audit

### Rituals Check
- [ ] Monday kickoff/goals prompt posted
- [ ] Mid-week engagement (Win Wednesday or similar)
- [ ] Weekly event held (call, workshop, etc.)
- [ ] Friday reflection or recap posted
- [ ] Weekend light engagement (optional)

### Member Activity
- [ ] New introductions posted
- [ ] Active members count stable/growing
- [ ] Diverse members participating (not same 10 people)
- [ ] Questions being asked
- [ ] Resources being consumed

### Events
- [ ] Weekly event scheduled
- [ ] Event promoted in advance
- [ ] Good attendance rate
- [ ] Post-event follow-up done
- [ ] Recording shared (if applicable)

### Content
- [ ] Content calendar followed
- [ ] Content quality maintained
- [ ] Content relevant to member needs
- [ ] Variety in content types
- [ ] Member feedback incorporated

## Engagement Red Flags

### Warning Signs to Watch
- [ ] Declining daily active users
- [ ] Same 10 people always posting
- [ ] Questions going unanswered
- [ ] Events poorly attended
- [ ] Negative sentiment increasing
- [ ] Introductions decreasing
- [ ] Churn increasing
- [ ] Feedback complaints rising

### If Red Flags Present
1. Survey members for feedback
2. Review recent changes
3. Increase founder presence
4. Refresh content approach
5. Add new engagement mechanic
6. Personal outreach to inactive members
