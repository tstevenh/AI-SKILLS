# SEO Copywriting Skill

Master the art of writing content that ranks on Google AND AI search engines while converting readers into customers.

## What This Skill Covers

This comprehensive skill teaches you to write content that:
- Ranks on Google's first page
- Gets cited by ChatGPT, Perplexity, and AI Overviews
- Converts readers into leads and customers
- Builds authority in your niche
- Attracts backlinks naturally

## Core Philosophy

**SEO copywriting is the balance between two audiences:**
1. Search engines (Google, AI systems) that determine visibility
2. Human readers who determine conversion

Content that only optimizes for search engines sounds robotic and doesn't convert.
Content that only optimizes for readers never gets discovered.

The goal: **Write for humans first, optimize for machines second.**

## Key Insight from Jacky Chou's Research

> "43.8% of ChatGPT citations come from 'Best X' listicles. 79.1% of cited content was updated in the current year. LLM PBNs + guest posts = #1 ranking in 10 days."

This means:
- Format matters (listicles dominate AI citations)
- Freshness matters (update content regularly)
- Authority signals work (backlinks still move the needle)

## The 2026 SEO Landscape

### What Changed
- **Helpful Content System** merged into core ranking (March 2024)
- **AI Overviews** rolled out to 100+ countries (2024-2025)
- **E-E-A-T** emphasis increased (Experience added 2022)
- **Zero-click searches** increased (answer in SERP)
- **AI search** now captures 15-20% of informational queries

### What Still Works
- Backlinks (Google confirmed: still top 3 factor)
- Content quality (always will matter)
- Matching search intent (foundational)
- Page experience (Core Web Vitals)
- Freshness (for relevant queries)

### What No Longer Works
- Keyword stuffing (never did, really)
- Thin content at scale
- Ignoring E-E-A-T signals
- Purely AI-generated content without human oversight
- Ignoring AI search optimization

## Core Principles

### 1. Search Intent is King
Every piece of content must match what users actually want. If Google shows listicles for a keyword, write a listicle. If they show how-to guides, write a how-to guide.

### 2. E-E-A-T is Non-Negotiable
- **Experience**: Show you've actually used/done what you write about
- **Expertise**: Demonstrate deep knowledge
- **Authoritativeness**: Build reputation through backlinks and mentions
- **Trust**: Be accurate, cite sources, be transparent

### 3. Structure for Scanning
Users don't readвҖ”they scan. Structure content with:
- Clear H2/H3 hierarchy
- Short paragraphs (2-3 sentences max)
- Bullet points and numbered lists
- Visual breaks every 300 words

### 4. Write for Both Google and AI
Google ranks pages. AI extracts and cites information.
- Traditional SEO: keyword placement, backlinks, technical optimization
- AI SEO: clear definitions, structured data, fresh content, "Best X" format

### 5. Conversion is the Goal
Rankings without conversions are vanity metrics. Every piece of content should:
- Have a clear CTA
- Move readers toward a goal
- Be part of a larger funnel
