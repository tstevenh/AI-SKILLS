## The Repurposing Philosophy

### The Content Efficiency Mindset

Most creators work too hard:
- Create unique content for every platform
- Start from scratch each time
- Exhaust themselves with volume requirements

Smart creators work efficiently:
- Create one core piece of content
- Extract maximum value from that piece
- Distribute strategically across platforms

### Why Repurposing Works

**Same message, different formats**:
- Some people prefer video
- Some people prefer reading
- Some people prefer audio
- Some people prefer short-form
- Reach all of them with one effort

**Multiple touchpoints**:
- Viewers rarely buy/subscribe on first touch
- Multiple exposures increase familiarity
- Different platforms = different discovery paths

**SEO multiplication**:
- Blog posts rank in Google
- Shorts get discovered differently than long-form
- Podcasts appear in different searches
- More content = more surface area

### The 1-to-20 Concept

From ONE 15-minute video, you can create:
1. The original long-form video
2-4. 3 YouTube Shorts
5-7. 3 TikToks/Reels
8. 1 blog post
9. 1 Twitter/X thread
10. 1 LinkedIn article
11-15. 5 LinkedIn posts
16. 1 podcast episode
17. 1 newsletter issue
18-20. 3 quote graphics
21+. Carousels, audiograms, clips...

## The Content Multiplication Framework

### The Content Hierarchy

```
                    ┌──────────────┐
                    │   LONG-FORM  │
                    │   VIDEO      │
                    │   (Core)     │
                    └──────┬───────┘
                           │
        ┌──────────────────┼──────────────────┐
        │                  │                  │
        ▼                  ▼                  ▼
   ┌─────────┐       ┌──────────┐       ┌──────────┐
   │ Short   │       │ Written  │       │  Audio   │
   │ Video   │       │ Content  │       │ Content  │
   └────┬────┘       └────┬─────┘       └────┬─────┘
        │                 │                  │
   ┌────┴────┐       ┌────┴────┐        ┌───┴───┐
   │         │       │         │        │       │
   ▼         ▼       ▼         ▼        ▼       ▼
Shorts   TikTok   Blog     Twitter   Podcast  Audiogram
Reels    Clips    Posts    Thread    Clips
```

### Content Atom Strategy

**Core content**: Your long-form video
**Content atoms**: Small, standalone pieces extracted from core

Each "atom" should:
- Make sense standalone
- Deliver value without context
- Link back to full content when appropriate
- Be optimized for its specific platform

## Long-Form to Shorts

### Identifying Short-Worthy Moments

Watch your long-form video and mark:
- **Golden moments**: Most insightful points
- **Emotional peaks**: Funniest, most dramatic moments
- **Standalone tips**: Individual pieces of advice
- **Quotable lines**: Memorable statements
- **Demonstrations**: Specific how-to segments

### Extraction Criteria

A good Short candidate:
- ✅ Makes sense without context
- ✅ Delivers value in 15-60 seconds
- ✅ Has strong opening hook
- ✅ Has clear ending/punchline
- ✅ Doesn't require seeing the full video

### Short Formats from Long-Form

**The Tip Extract**:
"Here's the #1 tip from my video on [topic]..."
[30-60 second valuable tip]

**The Highlight Reel**:
"3 things you need to know about [topic]..."
[Quick cuts between key points]

**The Story Moment**:
[Dramatic or emotional moment from video]
"Full video in profile..."

**The Counter-Intuitive Point**:
"Everyone says [common belief], but actually..."
[Your contrarian insight]

### Technical Adaptation

**What to change for Shorts**:
- Re-frame for vertical (9:16)
- Add captions
- Punch up the opening (first 1 second crucial)
- Remove any "as I mentioned earlier" references
- Add stronger ending

**What to keep**:
- Your energy and personality
- The core insight
- Visual demonstrations
- Authentic moments

## Video to Written Content

### Video to Blog Post

**The transcription method**:
1. Get transcript (YouTube auto-captions or service)
2. Clean up the transcript
3. Add headers and structure
4. Add images (screenshots, graphics)
5. Optimize for SEO
6. Add links and resources

**Blog post structure**:
```
# [Title optimized for SEO]

## Introduction
[Summary of what the video covers]

## [Point 1]
[Expanded content from video]

## [Point 2]
[Expanded content from video]

## [Point 3]
[Expanded content from video]

## Conclusion
[Summary + embedded video]

[Links to related content]
```

### Video to Twitter/X Thread

**Thread structure**:
```
Tweet 1: Hook (same as video hook, adapted)
Tweet 2-7: Key points (one per tweet)
Tweet 8: Summary or conclusion
Tweet 9: CTA + link to full video
```

**Thread tips**:
- Open with your strongest point
- Make each tweet standalone value
- Use numbers and lists
- End with link to video

### Video to LinkedIn Article

**LinkedIn article structure**:
- Professional framing
- Longer form than tweets
- Add business context
- Include personal lessons
- Embed video at end

### Video to Newsletter

**Newsletter format**:
```
Subject: [Curiosity hook from video]

Hi [Name],

[Personal intro relating to topic]

[3-5 key takeaways, formatted as bullet points]

[Link to video for full details]

[Personal sign-off]
```

## Video to Audio Content

### Video to Podcast Episode

**Full audio extraction**:
1. Export audio from video
2. Add podcast intro/outro
3. Remove any "as you can see on screen" references
4. Enhance audio quality
5. Add chapter markers
6. Distribute to podcast platforms

**Considerations**:
- Does content work without visual?
- Do you reference on-screen content?
- Is audio quality sufficient?

### Creating Audiograms

**Audiogram content**:
- 30-60 second audio clip
- Waveform visualization
- Captions
- Branding

**Best moments for audiograms**:
- Quotable statements
- Insightful moments
- Emotional content
- Counter-intuitive points

## Video to Social Content

### Quote Graphics

**Extract quotable moments**:
"The algorithm doesn't hate you. You hate the algorithm."

**Create graphics with**:
- Your quote
- Your branding
- Photo or simple design
- Consistent template

### Carousel Posts

**Carousel structure** (Instagram/LinkedIn):
- Slide 1: Hook/title
- Slides 2-6: Key points
- Slide 7: Summary/CTA

**Content from video**:
- Each main point = one slide
- Screenshots as visuals
- Simple text + graphics

### Story Content

**From video to Stories**:
- Behind-the-scenes of filming
- Teaser clips
- Poll about video topic
- Q&A based on video questions

## The Complete Repurposing Workflow

### Workflow Overview

```
Day 0: Publish video
Day 0-1: Extract Shorts (while content fresh)
Day 1: Create Twitter thread
Day 2: Write blog post
Day 3: Create LinkedIn content
Day 4: Prepare newsletter section
Day 5: Create quote graphics
Day 6: Create audiogram
Ongoing: Schedule and publish
```

### The Repurposing Sprint

**Option A: Same-day sprint**
After filming, immediately:
1. Note timestamps for Shorts
2. Outline blog post
3. Draft thread
4. Delegate or batch create

**Option B: Post-publish sprint**
After video publishes:
1. Review for best moments
2. Extract Shorts
3. Create written content
4. Schedule across platforms

### Documentation Template

For each video, document:

## Repurposing Best Practices

### Do:
- Adapt content for each platform
- Maintain consistent branding
- Link back to original when appropriate
- Track what performs where
- Batch repurposing tasks

### Don't:
- Copy-paste without adaptation
- Overwhelm one platform with content
- Forget to optimize for each format
- Ignore platform-specific norms
- Repurpose content that didn't perform

### The 80/20 Rule

**Focus repurposing on your best content**:
- Top-performing videos deserve maximum repurposing
- Average videos get basic treatment
- Poor performers may not be worth repurposing
