## Channel Overview Audit

### Baseline Metrics

**Gather these numbers for the last 90 days**:

```
CHANNEL BASELINE: [Date]

OVERALL METRICS:
├── Total views: ____________
├── Watch time (hours): ____________
├── Subscribers (net): ____________
├── Videos published: ____________
└── Average views per video: ____________

TREND vs PREVIOUS 90 DAYS:
├── Views: ________% change
├── Watch time: ________% change
├── Subscribers: ________% change
└── Videos published: ________ change

HEALTH INDICATORS:
├── Views per subscriber: ____________
├── Watch time per subscriber: ____________
├── Subscriber growth rate: ________%
└── Average views first 48h: ____________
```

### Trend Analysis Checklist

- [ ] Is total viewership increasing, stable, or declining?
- [ ] Is watch time growing proportionally with views?
- [ ] Is subscriber growth healthy relative to views?
- [ ] Are you publishing consistently?
- [ ] Are individual videos improving over time?

### Red Flags to Check

- [ ] Subscriber count rising but views flat (zombie subscribers)
- [ ] Watch time declining while views stable (engagement problem)
- [ ] Views per video declining over time
- [ ] Long gaps between uploads
- [ ] Inconsistent performance (high variance)
