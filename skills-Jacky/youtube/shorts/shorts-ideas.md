## Execution Tips

### Making These Ideas Your Own

1. **Adapt to your niche**: Replace [brackets] with your specific topic
2. **Add personality**: Bring your unique energy and perspective
3. **Test variations**: Same idea, different angles
4. **Track what works**: Note which formats perform best

### Production Efficiency

- Batch film 5-10 Shorts in one session
- Create templates for recurring formats
- Repurpose long-form content
- Save trending sounds for future use

### Content Calendar Integration

- Plan 7-14 Shorts per week
- Mix categories for variety
- Schedule trend-responsive slots
- Reserve some for timely content
