# Service Business Sequences (5 Complete Sequences)

Ready-to-use sequences for consulting, coaching, professional services, and B2B services companies.

---

## Sequence 1: Consulting Services

**Use Case**: Management consulting, strategy consulting, specialized advisory
**Length**: 5 emails over 21 days

### Email 1 - Day 1
```
Subject: Thought about [Company]'s [challenge/opportunity]

[First name],

[Personalized observation about their business, industry position, or recent news]

Most [company types] in your situation face [specific challenge]. The typical approaches [Problem with typical approaches].

I've helped companies like [Similar Company 1] and [Similar Company 2] navigate this differently—resulting in [outcome].

Would a 20-minute conversation to explore whether that applies here be useful?

[Your name]
[Title], [Firm]
```

### Email 2 - Day 5
```
Subject: Re: Thought about [Company]'s [challenge]

[First name],

Following up on my note about [specific challenge].

Quick example: [Similar Company] faced [same challenge]. We [approach], and they [result].

The situation at [Company] seems similar based on [observation].

Worth a quick call to discuss?
```

### Email 3 - Day 10
```
Subject: Framework for [challenge]

[First name],

Wanted to share a framework we use for companies facing [challenge].

[Brief framework description or key insight]

It's helped [X] companies navigate this successfully.

Would it be useful to walk through how it could apply to [Company]?
```

### Email 4 - Day 16
```
Subject: [Similar Company]'s approach

[First name],

Brief case study that might be relevant:

**[Similar Company]** faced [challenge]. After [timeframe] working together:
- [Result 1]
- [Result 2]  
- [Result 3]

Their [title] said: "[Brief quote]"

The approach is transferable. Worth exploring for [Company]?
```

### Email 5 - Day 21
```
Subject: Closing thoughts

[First name],

I've reached out a few times about [challenge]. Not hearing back likely means it's not a priority right now—totally understand.

If things change, I'm here. We've helped [X] companies similar to [Company] with exactly this.

Best of luck with everything.
```

### Email 3 - Day 10
```
Subject: [Industry] talent insight

[First name],

[Insight about talent market, salary trends, availability]

### Email 4 - Day 15
```
Subject: Guarantee on placement

[First name],

Here's what differentiates us: [guarantee—replacement guarantee, satisfaction terms, etc.].

## Customization Guidelines

### For All Service Sequences
- Research their specific situation thoroughly
- Reference relevant case studies
- Adjust formality based on industry norms
- Include relevant credentials/certifications

### Proof Points
- Quantify results where possible
- Include client names (with permission)
- Add testimonials/quotes
- Reference relevant experience

### Industry Adjustments
- B2B services: More formal, longer sales cycles
- SMB services: More direct, faster decisions
- Enterprise services: Multi-stakeholder, relationship-focused

---

*Service businesses sell expertise and relationships. These sequences open doors—the real work happens in conversation.*
