# Content Audit Checklist: Monthly Strategy Review

## Overview

A monthly content audit is essential for sustainable growth. This systematic review helps you understand what's working, what isn't, and how to optimize your strategy going forward.

Schedule 2-3 hours monthly for this audit. The insights generated will save countless hours of creating content that doesn't resonate.

---

## Pre-Audit Preparation

### Gather Your Data

Before starting, collect:

- [ ] Platform analytics for the past 30 days
- [ ] Spreadsheet or document for notes
- [ ] List of all content posted this month
- [ ] Previous month's audit for comparison
- [ ] Quiet time without distractions

Create columns for:
- Post date

## Quick Reference Checklist

### Monthly Audit Quick Version (30 min)

If time is limited, at minimum:

- [ ] Identify top 3 and bottom 3 performers
- [ ] Note common patterns in both
- [ ] Check follower growth trend
- [ ] Review comment themes

- [ ] Identify 1 thing to change

### Quarterly Deep Dive (Additional)

Every quarter, also review:

- [ ] Overall growth trajectory
- [ ] Monetization progress
- [ ] Brand perception
- [ ] Platform algorithm changes
- [ ] Long-term strategy alignment
- [ ] Tool and workflow optimization
- [ ] Team/resource needs

---

## Final Notes

### Audit Mindset

**Be Honest:**
The audit only works if you're honest about what's working and what isn't.

**Be Curious:**
Ask "why" repeatedly. Surface-level analysis misses insights.

**Be Action-Oriented:**
Insights without action are useless. Commit to changes.

**Be Patient:**
One month isn't always enough to judge. Look for patterns over time.

### Remember

The goal of the audit is improvement, not perfection. Use insights to get better incrementally. Small improvements compound into significant growth over time.
