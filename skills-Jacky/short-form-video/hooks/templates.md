## How to Use These Templates

### Step 1: Choose Based on Content Type
- Educational → Question, List, Mistake, Bold Claim
- Entertainment → Relatable, Story, Demonstration  
- Inspirational → Transformation, Story, Bold Claim
- Controversial → Controversy, Bold Claim, Mistake

### Step 2: Fill in the Blanks
Replace bracketed text with your specific topic, making it concrete and specific.

**Template:** "The [number] things that [outcome]"
**Filled:** "The 5 habits that doubled my productivity"

### Step 3: Add Specificity
Generic hooks fail. Specific hooks convert.

**Weak:** "How to make more money"
**Strong:** "How I made $5,000 in 30 days without working weekends"

### Step 4: Test Variations
Try different hooks for similar content and track what works.

### Step 5: Build Your Personal Library
Over time, identify which templates work best for YOUR audience and niche.
