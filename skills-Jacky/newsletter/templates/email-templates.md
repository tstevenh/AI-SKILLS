# Email Templates: Copy-and-Paste Collection

Ready-to-use email templates for every newsletter situation.

---

## Announcement Emails

### New Feature/Change Announcement

**Subject:** Something new in [Newsletter Name]

```
Hey [Name],

I've got something to share.

Starting [date], [Newsletter Name] is getting [change]:

WHAT'S CHANGING:
[Clear explanation of the change]

WHY:
[Reason for the change - be honest]

WHAT THIS MEANS FOR YOU:
• [Impact 1]
• [Impact 2]
• [Impact 3]

WHAT STAYS THE SAME:
[Reassurance about what they love]

Questions? Just reply to this email.

[Your name]

P.S. [Optional additional note]
```

### Price Increase Announcement

**Subject:** Important update about [Newsletter Name]

```
[Name],

I wanted to give you a heads up.

On [date], the price of [product/subscription] is 
changing from $[old] to $[new].

WHY THE CHANGE:
[Honest explanation - costs, value, etc.]

WHAT YOU GET:
Since you're already a subscriber, you're locked in 
at your current rate. No action needed.

FOR NEW SUBSCRIBERS:
The new pricing reflects the value we've added:
• [Value add 1]
• [Value add 2]
• [Value add 3]

Thank you for being part of this community.

[Your name]

P.S. If you know anyone who might want to subscribe 
before the change, they have until [date].
```

### Hiatus/Break Announcement

**Subject:** Taking a short break

```
Hey [Name],

I wanted to let you know I'm taking a short break from 
[Newsletter Name].

WHY:
[Be honest - vacation, personal, creative recharge, etc.]

WHEN I'LL BE BACK:
[Date] — mark your calendar!

IN THE MEANTIME:
• [What they can do/read]
• [Archive link]
• [Other resources]

Thank you for understanding. See you soon.

[Your name]
```

## Engagement Emails

### Feedback Request

**Subject:** Quick question for you

```
[Name],

I have a favor to ask.

I'm working on making [Newsletter Name] better, and 
I'd love your input.

What's ONE thing you wish I covered more of?

Just hit reply and let me know. Even a few words helps.

Thanks for reading,
[Your name]
```

### Survey Request

**Subject:** Help shape [Newsletter Name] (2 min)

```
[Name],

Once a year, I ask readers what they think.

Would you take 2 minutes to share your feedback?

[TAKE THE SURVEY]

Your responses help me:
• Create better content
• Focus on what matters to you
• Make decisions about the newsletter

As a thank you, everyone who completes the survey 
gets [incentive].

[Your name]

P.S. Survey closes [date].
```

### Thank You Email

**Subject:** Thank you

```

[Reason for thank you - milestone, feedback, support, etc.]

This newsletter exists because of readers like you. 
I don't take that for granted.

Here's to what's next.

Grateful,
[Your name]
```

## Re-engagement Emails

### "Miss You" Email

**Subject:** Is this thing on?

```
Hey [Name],

I noticed you haven't opened my emails in a while.

No pressure — just wanted to check in.

If you're still interested in [topic]:
[CLICK HERE TO STAY SUBSCRIBED]

If your interests have changed, that's okay too. 
You can unsubscribe at the bottom of this email.

Either way, thanks for being here while you were.

[Your name]
```

## Quick Response Templates

### To a Positive Reply

```
[Name],

Thank you so much for this message — it really made my day.

Feedback like yours is exactly why I keep writing.

[Personal response to their specific comment]

Appreciate you!

[Your name]
```

### To a Suggestion

```
[Name],

Thank you for the suggestion!

[Your response — whether you'll implement, consider, or explain why not]

I always appreciate reader input — it makes the newsletter better.

[Your name]
```

### To a Complaint

```
[Name],

I appreciate you taking the time to share this feedback.

[Acknowledge their concern]

[Your response/explanation/apology if warranted]

[What you'll do or why things are the way they are]

Thanks for reading despite [issue]. I'll work to do better.

[Your name]
```

- [ ] Replace all [brackets] with your info
- [ ] Adjust tone to match your voice
- [ ] Add specific details/examples
- [ ] Read aloud for flow
- [ ] Test all links
- [ ] Preview on mobile
