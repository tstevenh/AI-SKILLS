## Customization Guide

### Adjusting for Your Size

**Small Newsletter (1-10k):**
- Focus on engagement rates
- Highlight audience quality
- Lower price points
- Emphasize growth trajectory

**Medium Newsletter (10-50k):**
- Balance reach and engagement
- Include demographic data
- Competitive pricing
- Case studies if available

**Large Newsletter (50k+):**
- Lead with scale
- Detailed demographics
- Premium pricing
- Robust social proof

### Pricing Your Media Kit

**CPM Calculation:**
```
Price = (Subscribers × Open Rate × CPM) / 1,000

Example:
25,000 × 0.42 × $50 = $525 per send
```

**CPM by Niche:**
| Niche | CPM Range |
|-------|-----------|
| General | $20-40 |
| Business | $35-60 |
| Tech | $45-75 |
| Finance | $55-90 |
| B2B Professional | $70-120 |

### Design Tips

1. **Clean layout** - White space is your friend
2. **Consistent branding** - Use your colors/fonts
3. **Visual data** - Charts > tables when possible
4. **Mobile-friendly** - PDFs should work on phones
5. **Easy to share** - Reasonable file size

### Format Options

| Format | Pros | Cons |
|--------|------|------|
| PDF | Professional, shareable | Static |
| Notion page | Interactive, updatable | Less formal |
| Website page | Always current | Harder to share |
| Google Doc | Easy collaboration | Less polished |
