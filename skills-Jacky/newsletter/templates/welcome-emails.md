# Welcome Email Templates: Ready-to-Use Sequences

Copy, customize, and deploy these welcome email templates for your newsletter. Each is tested and optimized for engagement.

---

## Template Set 1: The Warm Professional

Best for: B2B, professional, expert newsletters

### Email 1: The Warm Welcome (Immediate)

**Subject:** Welcome to [Newsletter Name] — let's get started

```
Hi [First Name],

Welcome! You've just joined [X] [professionals/marketers/founders] 
who read [Newsletter Name].

I'm [Your Name], and I've been [your credibility: working in X 
for Y years / built Z / etc.].

Here's what to expect:
• Every [frequency], you'll get [what they get]
• Each issue takes about [X] minutes to read
• I focus on [your unique angle]

**Quick favor:** To make sure you receive every issue, add 
[your email] to your contacts or move this email to Primary.

Tomorrow, I'll share [teaser of next email]. Don't miss it.

Best,
[Your name]
[Your title/company]

P.S. Have a question? Just hit reply — I read every email.
```

## Implementation Guide

### Timing Recommendations

| Email | Send Timing |
|-------|-------------|
| Email 1 | Immediate (within 5 minutes) |
| Email 2 | 24 hours after signup |
| Email 3 | 48 hours after signup |
| Email 4 | 4 days after signup |
| Email 5 | 7 days after signup |

### Customization Checklist

For each email:
- [ ] Replace [Newsletter Name]
- [ ] Replace [Your Name]
- [ ] Replace [First Name] merge tag
- [ ] Insert actual frequency
- [ ] Add your credibility/story
- [ ] Insert your resources/links
- [ ] Add your referral link
- [ ] Adjust tone to match brand
- [ ] Add any specific CTAs
- [ ] Test all links

### Testing Your Sequence

1. Sign up with a test email
2. Receive each email on schedule
3. Check formatting on desktop and mobile
4. Verify all links work
5. Read for flow and consistency
6. Adjust based on feedback

### Metrics to Track

| Email | Target Open Rate | Target Click Rate |
|-------|-----------------|-------------------|
| Email 1 | 60-70% | 20-30% |
| Email 2 | 50-60% | 15-25% |
| Email 3 | 45-55% | 20-35% |
| Email 4 | 40-50% | 15-25% |
| Email 5 | 40-50% | 10-20% |

### Iteration Tips

- A/B test subject lines
- Monitor reply rates (especially Email 4)
- Track referral conversions (Email 5)
- Survey new subscribers after 30 days
- Update content quarterly
