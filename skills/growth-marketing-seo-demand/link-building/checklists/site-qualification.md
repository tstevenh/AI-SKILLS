# Site Qualification Checklist

## Quick Filter (30 Seconds)

### Instant Checks
- [ ] **DR/DA**: Above campaign minimum? (Standard: 30+)
- [ ] **Traffic**: Has real organic traffic? (Min: 500+)
- [ ] **Language**: Appropriate for target market?
- [ ] **Spam signals**: Any obvious red flags?

**If ANY fail → Skip immediately**

---

## Full Qualification (5 Minutes)

### Domain Metrics
- [ ] DR meets threshold: ___
- [ ] Organic traffic: ___ (verified in Ahrefs/SEMrush)
- [ ] Domain age: ___ years
- [ ] TF/CF ratio: ___ (0.5+ ideal)

### Traffic Verification
- [ ] Traffic source breakdown checked
- [ ] Traffic from relevant countries
- [ ] Traffic trend stable or growing
- [ ] Traffic keywords match site content

### Content Quality
- [ ] Reviewed 3-5 recent articles
- [ ] Writing quality acceptable
- [ ] Content is original (not spun/AI junk)
- [ ] Regular publishing schedule
- [ ] Engaged audience (comments, shares)

### Editorial Standards
- [ ] Site has content guidelines
- [ ] Evidence of editorial review
- [ ] Not every post is "guest" or "sponsored"
- [ ] Contact information available

### Link Profile (Inbound)
- [ ] Links from recognizable sites
- [ ] No obvious spam links dominating
- [ ] Natural DR distribution
- [ ] Relevant linking sites

### Link Profile (Outbound)
- [ ] Outbound links go to quality sites
- [ ] No links to gambling/pharma/adult
- [ ] Reasonable outbound link count per post
- [ ] Links are relevant to content

### Relevance
- [ ] Topically relevant to campaign (score 1-5): ___
- [ ] Audience match to target customers
- [ ] Content fits target topic area

---

## Red Flags (Instant Rejection)

### Immediate Disqualifiers
- [ ] DR below 15
- [ ] Zero organic traffic with high DR
- [ ] Site is deindexed (site:domain.com = no results)
- [ ] Obvious PBN characteristics
- [ ] Spam content (casino, pharma, adult)
- [ ] Site accepts any/all content
- [ ] Recently expired domain (archive.org check)
- [ ] Link neighborhood includes spam

---

## Scoring Framework

Rate each factor 1-5:

| Factor | Weight | Score | Weighted |
|--------|--------|-------|----------|
| DR/DA | 20% | ___ | ___ |
| Traffic | 20% | ___ | ___ |
| Content Quality | 20% | ___ | ___ |
| Relevance | 15% | ___ | ___ |
| Editorial Standards | 15% | ___ | ___ |
| Link Profile | 10% | ___ | ___ |
| **TOTAL** | 100% | | **___** |

### Score Interpretation
- **4.0-5.0**: Premium opportunity - Prioritize
- **3.5-3.9**: Quality opportunity - Pursue
- **3.0-3.4**: Acceptable - Standard campaigns
- **Below 3.0**: Skip or reconsider
