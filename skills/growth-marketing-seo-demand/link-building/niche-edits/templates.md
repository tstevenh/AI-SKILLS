# Niche Edit Email Templates (15+)

## How to Use These Templates

Niche edit outreach is more transactional than guest posting. Keep emails:
- **Short** - Get to the point quickly
- **Specific** - Reference the exact article
- **Clear** - State exactly what you want
- **Value-oriented** - Explain what's in it for them

**Variables to replace:**
- `[Name]` - Contact's name
- `[Site Name]` - Their website
- `[Article Title]` - Title of target article
- `[Article URL]` - Link to target article
- `[Your Resource]` - What you want linked
- `[Your URL]` - Your target URL
- `[Anchor Text]` - Preferred anchor
- `[Section]` - Specific section reference
- `[$X]` - Price point

## Standard Outreach Templates

### Template 1: Direct Paid Placement Request

**Subject:** Link placement inquiry - [Article Title]

```
Hi [Name],

I came across your article "[Article Title]" and think it would be a great fit for a mention of our resource on [topic].

We have a small budget for placements like this. Would you be open to adding a contextual link?

Happy to share more details or discuss rates if you're interested.

Thanks,
[Your Name]
```

### Template 2: Value-Add Pitch (No Payment Mentioned)

**Subject:** Suggestion for your [Topic] article

```
Hi [Name],

I was reading your article on [Article Title] and noticed it covers [topics A, B, C] really well.

One thing your readers might also find useful is [your resource/topic]. It addresses [specific benefit], which complements what you've already written.

Would you be open to adding a brief mention? I can provide suggested text if helpful.

Thanks,
[Your Name]
```

### Template 3: Content Gap Approach

**Subject:** Quick suggestion for [Article Title]

```
Hi [Name],

Great article on [topic] - it's one of the more comprehensive guides I've seen.

I noticed you mention [related topics] but don't cover [your topic]. Given that many readers interested in [subject] are also looking for [your solution], it might be worth a quick addition.

We have a resource at [Your URL] that covers this well. Would you consider adding it?

Let me know what you think.

[Your Name]
```

### Template 4: "Best Of" List Inclusion

**Subject:** Inclusion request for your [Product Category] list

```
Hi [Name],

I saw your list of best [product category] and thought [Your Product] might be a good fit.

We [brief differentiator - what makes you unique]. Since your list already features [competitors/similar products], I think we'd complement them well.

Would you consider adding us? Happy to provide any details you need for a write-up.

Thanks,
[Your Name]
```

### Template 5: Resource Page Request

**Subject:** Resource suggestion for [Page Title]

```
Hi [Name],

I found your resource page on [topic] and wanted to suggest an addition.

[Your resource] is [brief description with key benefit]. I think it would be helpful alongside the other resources you've curated.

Here's the link: [Your URL]

Would this fit your page?

Best,
[Your Name]
```

## Broken Link Templates

**Subject:** Broken link in your [Topic] article

```

We have a similar resource at [Your URL] that covers [topic]. If you'd like to update the broken link, ours could be a good replacement.

Let me know if you'd like to take a look.

Thanks,
[Your Name]
```

```

I have two options that might help:

1. We have a resource at [Your URL] that covers similar content
2. If you prefer, I could help you find an alternative that matches what was there originally

Just want to help fix the issue - let me know how I can help.

[Your Name]
```

## Negotiation Templates

### Template 10: Response to Pricing Inquiry

**Subject:** Re: Link placement inquiry

```
Hi [Name],

Thanks for getting back to me!

We typically budget around $[X]-$[Y] for placements like this, depending on the specifics. Does that range work for you?

Happy to discuss details.

[Your Name]
```

### Template 11: Counter-Offer

**Subject:** Re: Link placement inquiry

```
Hi [Name],

Thanks for sharing your rates. $[Their Price] is a bit above our budget for this campaign.

Would $[Your Offer] work? If we could do 2-3 placements over time, we might be able to justify a higher per-placement rate.

Let me know your thoughts.

[Your Name]
```

### Template 12: Volume Commitment Offer

**Subject:** Re: Link placement inquiry

```
Hi [Name],

Before we finalize - we're doing link placements across several articles in [your industry].

If we committed to 3 placements on [Site Name], would you be able to offer a better rate per placement?

Thanks,
[Your Name]
```

## Quick Tips

1. **Be specific** - Always reference the exact article
2. **Keep it short** - 50-100 words for initial outreach
3. **State your ask clearly** - Don't be vague
4. **Offer value** - Even if paying, frame as beneficial to them
5. **Make it easy** - Provide suggested text/anchor
6. **Follow up** - But no more than 3 times
7. **Be professional** - This is a business transaction
