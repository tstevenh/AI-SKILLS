## GUEST POST TEMPLATES (1-10)

### Template 1: Standard Professional

**Subject:** Guest post contribution for [Site Name]

```
Hi [Name],

I've been following [Site Name] for a while and really appreciate your coverage of [topic area]. Your recent article on [specific article] was particularly insightful.

I'd love to contribute a guest post on:

"[Proposed Title]"

This piece would cover:
• [Point 1]
• [Point 2]
• [Point 3]

I've previously written for [Publication 1] and [Publication 2] on similar topics.

Would this be of interest? Happy to send a detailed outline.

Best,
[Your Name]
```

### Template 2: Short and Direct

**Subject:** Guest post idea for [Site Name]

```
Hi [Name],

I have a guest post idea for [Site Name]:

"[Title]"

It would cover [1-2 sentence summary]. I've written similar pieces for [Publication].

Worth exploring?

[Your Name]
```

## USAGE TIPS

1. **Always personalize** - Templates are starting points
2. **Match tone** - Formal for corporates, casual for blogs
3. **Keep short** - Under 150 words ideal
4. **One ask** - Don't confuse with multiple requests
5. **Test and iterate** - Track what works
6. **Proofread** - Errors kill credibility
