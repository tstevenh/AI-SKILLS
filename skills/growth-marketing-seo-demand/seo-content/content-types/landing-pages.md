# SEO Landing Pages: Conversion-Focused Content

> Landing pages rank for transactional and commercial keywords while driving conversions. They're where SEO meets revenue.

## What is an SEO Landing Page?

An SEO landing page is a conversion-focused page optimized to rank organically for specific keywords. Unlike PPC landing pages (traffic from ads), SEO landing pages must satisfy both search engines AND convert visitors.

**The challenge**: Balancing SEO requirements (comprehensive content) with conversion goals (focused messaging and CTAs).

---

## SEO Landing Page vs. Blog Post

| Aspect | SEO Landing Page | Blog Post |
|--------|------------------|-----------|
| **Primary goal** | Conversion | Education |
| **Keyword intent** | Commercial/Transactional | Informational |
| **CTA focus** | Heavy (primary action) | Light (secondary actions) |
| **Content style** | Benefits-focused | Information-focused |
| **Length** | 800-2,500 words | 1,500-5,000+ words |
| **Format** | Scannable sections | Long-form reading |

## When to Use SEO Landing Pages

### Target Keywords

Landing pages work for:

| Intent | Example Keywords |
|--------|-----------------|
| Commercial | "best CRM software," "[product] alternatives" |
| Transactional | "[product] pricing," "[product] free trial" |
| Feature-specific | "CRM with email integration" |
| Use-case specific | "CRM for real estate agents" |
| Location-based | "[service] in [city]" |

### Business Goals

Create landing pages when you need:
- Pages that rank AND convert
- Programmatic SEO at scale
- Location pages for local SEO
- Feature pages for product marketing
- Category pages for e-commerce

## The Headline

### Primary Headline (H1)

Combine SEO keyword with compelling value proposition:

```markdown
❌ SEO-only: "Local SEO Software"
❌ Conversion-only: "Grow Your Business Faster"
✅ Both: "Local SEO Software That Gets Your Business Found"
```

### Headline Formulas

```
[Product/Service] That [Key Benefit]
[Achieve Outcome] with [Product/Service]
The [Category] That [Differentiator]
```

### Examples

```
Local Rank Tracking That Shows Every Location
Write Better Content with AI-Powered SEO Tools
The Link Building Service That Actually Works
```

### Subheadline

Expand on the headline with supporting info:

```markdown
H1: Local SEO Software That Gets Your Business Found

Subheadline: Track rankings across all your locations, manage 
citations, and prove ROI to clients—in one dashboard.
```

## SEO Optimization

### Keyword Placement

| Location | Optimization |
|----------|-------------|
| Title tag | Primary keyword + benefit |
| H1 | Keyword + value proposition |
| URL | /primary-keyword or /category/primary-keyword |
| First 100 words | Include primary keyword naturally |
| H2s | Secondary keywords where natural |
| Alt text | Product screenshots with descriptive text |

### Content Length

Landing pages need enough content to rank:

- **Minimum**: 800 words
- **Typical**: 1,500-2,500 words
- **Maximum**: Keep it focused; don't bloat

### Internal Linking

Link to and from:
- Related blog posts
- Feature pages
- Case studies
- Comparison pages

## Landing Page Copywriting Tips

### Lead with Benefits

```markdown
❌ Feature-focused: "Grid-based rank tracking technology"
✅ Benefit-focused: "See exactly where you rank—down to the block"
```

### Use Specific Numbers

```markdown
❌ Vague: "Trusted by many agencies"
✅ Specific: "Trusted by 2,500+ agencies tracking 50,000+ locations"
```

### Address Objections

Anticipate and answer concerns:

```markdown
"But what if it's too complicated?"
→ "Get started in 3 minutes. No technical setup required."

"But what if it's too expensive?"
→ "Plans start at $49/mo—less than one hour of billable time."
```

### Use Social Proof Strategically

Place testimonials near CTAs and after benefit claims.

## Landing Page Checklist

Before publishing:

- [ ] Keyword in title tag and H1
- [ ] Clear value proposition above fold
- [ ] Primary CTA visible without scrolling
- [ ] Problem/solution framework
- [ ] Benefits > features
- [ ] Social proof (testimonials, logos, numbers)
- [ ] How it works section
- [ ] Clear pricing or offer
- [ ] FAQ addressing objections
- [ ] Multiple CTAs throughout
- [ ] Mobile-optimized
- [ ] Fast loading (Core Web Vitals pass)
- [ ] Schema markup added
