# Launch Email Templates

## Overview

A well-executed email launch sequence can make or break your community launch. These templates provide a proven framework for announcing, promoting, and closing your community launch.

---

## Pre-Launch Sequence

### Email 1: Teaser (7 days before)

```
Subject: Something new is coming...

Hey [Name],

I've been working on something for months.

And next [DAY], I'm finally ready to share it with you.

Here's what I can tell you:

It's for [TARGET AUDIENCE] who want [OUTCOME].

It's something I wish existed when I [YOUR STRUGGLE].

And if you've ever [PAIN POINT], this could change everything.

I'll have all the details on [DATE].

For now, I just wanted to give you a heads up—because 
you're going to want to be ready when the doors open.

More soon.

[Your Name]

P.S. Any guesses what it is? Hit reply—I'm curious!
```

## Summary

### Launch Email Best Practices

1. **Build Anticipation**: Tease before you launch
2. **Create Urgency**: Real deadlines drive action
3. **Show Social Proof**: Share results and testimonials
4. **Handle Objections**: Address concerns proactively
5. **Be Personal**: Tell your story, be authentic

### Launch Email Timeline

```
Day -7: Teaser
Day -3: Announcement
Day -1: Tomorrow reminder
Day 0 (AM): Launch!
Day 0 (PM): Update
Day 1: Social proof
Day 2: Objection handling
Day 3: Story/Why
Day 4: 48 hours
Day 5: 24 hours
Day 6: Final hours
Day 6: Last chance
Day 7: Thank you
```
