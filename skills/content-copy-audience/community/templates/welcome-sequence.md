# Welcome Sequence Templates

## Overview

A well-crafted welcome sequence sets new members up for success. These templates provide ready-to-use frameworks for welcoming members across email, platform, and personal touchpoints.

---

## Welcome Call Script

```
WELCOME CALL OUTLINE (30-60 minutes)

OPENING (5 min)
- Welcome everyone
- Quick housekeeping (mute, questions, recording)
- Today's agenda

INTRODUCTIONS (10-15 min)
- Go around: Name, location, one goal
- Keep brief (30 seconds each)

COMMUNITY OVERVIEW (10 min)
- Why this community exists
- What makes us different
- Community values
- What to expect

QUICK TOUR (10 min)
- Key areas to explore
- How to navigate
- Where to start

EXPECTATIONS (5 min)
- What you'll get
- What we expect (engagement, respect)
- How to get help

Q&A (10 min)
- Open floor
- Address common questions

CLOSE (5 min)
- Summary of first steps
- Next event
- Thank everyone
- Encouragement
```

## Summary

### Welcome Sequence Best Practices

1. **Be Personal**: Use their name, reference their intro
2. **Be Helpful**: Give clear, actionable guidance
3. **Be Timely**: Space messages appropriately
4. **Be Consistent**: Same quality for every member
5. **Be Available**: Make it easy to ask questions
