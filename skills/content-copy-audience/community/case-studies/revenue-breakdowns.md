# Revenue Breakdowns: Community Business Models in Practice

## Overview

Understanding real revenue numbers helps you plan your own community business. This guide breaks down typical revenue structures at different scales, showing you what's possible and what it takes to get there.

---

## $10K/Month Community

### Profile

```
MONTHLY REVENUE: $10,000

Members: 150-250
Price Point: $39-69/month
Platform: Skool or Circle (basic)
Team: Founder only or part-time VA
Time Investment: 15-25 hours/week
```

### Revenue Mix

```
Primary Revenue:
- Membership fees: $10,000 (100%)

Typical Breakdown by Model:

Model A: Single Tier
- 200 members × $50/month = $10,000

Model B: Two Tiers
- 150 members × $39/month = $5,850
- 50 members × $89/month = $4,450
Total: $10,300

Model C: Annual Focus
- 50 monthly × $49 = $2,450
- 150 annual × $399/year = $4,987/month
Total: ~$7,400 + additional sales
```

### Cost Structure

```
MONTHLY COSTS

Fixed Costs:
- Platform (Skool Pro): $99
- Email marketing: $50
- Tools/software: $100
Total Fixed: ~$250

Variable Costs:
- VA (if any): $500-1,000
- Content: $200 (optional)
- Marketing: $500-1,000
Total Variable: ~$1,000-2,000

TOTAL COSTS: $1,250-2,250
PROFIT: $7,750-8,750
MARGIN: 78-88%
```

### What It Takes

```
To reach $10K/month:

FROM SCRATCH:
- 6-12 months timeline
- Existing audience of 1,000+ email or 5,000+ social
- Consistent content for 6+ months
- 2-3% conversion from audience
- 90%+ retention rate needed

GROWTH PATH:
Month 1-2: Launch with 30-50 founding members
Month 3-4: Grow to 80-100 members
Month 5-6: Reach 130-150 members
Month 7-12: Scale to 200+ members

KEY SUCCESS FACTORS:
✓ Clear niche
✓ Existing audience
✓ Strong value proposition
✓ Good retention
✓ Consistent engagement
```

Operations:
- Platform: $200
- Tools/software: $300
- Email: $100
Total Ops: $600

Content/Events:
- Guest speakers: $500
- Event costs: $200
- Content creation: $500
Total Content: $1,200

Marketing:
- Ads: $2,000
- Partnerships: $500
Total Marketing: $2,500

TOTAL COSTS: ~$9,800
PROFIT: ~$40,000
MARGIN: 80%

### What It Takes

REQUIREMENTS:

## Revenue Optimization Strategies

### Increasing ARPU (Average Revenue Per User)

```
STRATEGY 1: Add Premium Tier
- Create VIP tier at 3x price
- Target 10% of members to upgrade
- Impact: +30% ARPU

STRATEGY 2: Annual Push
- Increase annual discount
- Target 50% annual mix
- Impact: +20% LTV, better cash flow

STRATEGY 3: Upsell Products
- Launch courses, workshops, coaching
- Target 20% participation
- Impact: +25% ARPU
```

### Reducing Churn

```
For every 1% churn reduction:

At $50K MRR:
- Monthly savings: $500
- Annual impact: $6,000+

At $100K MRR:
- Monthly savings: $1,000
- Annual impact: $12,000+

CHURN REDUCTION TACTICS:
1. Improve onboarding (+5-10% retention)
2. Add engagement rituals (+3-5%)
3. Build connections (+5-10%)
4. Personal outreach to at-risk (+2-5%)
```

### Scaling Acquisition

```
CAC Targets by Revenue Level:

$10K/month: CAC should be <$100
$50K/month: CAC should be <$150
$100K/month: CAC should be <$200

ACQUISITION MATH:
If LTV = $800
And target LTV:CAC = 4:1
Max CAC = $200

At $200 CAC:
$10K marketing → 50 new members
$50K marketing → 250 new members
```

## Summary

### Revenue Milestones

| Milestone | Members | Price | Monthly Revenue |
|-----------|---------|-------|-----------------|
| First $1K | 20-25 | $49 | $1,000 |
| First $10K | 150-250 | $49-69 | $10,000 |
| First $50K | 500-1,000 | $49-99 | $50,000 |
| First $100K | 1,000-2,500 | $49-199 | $100,000 |
| First $1M/yr | 800-2,000 | Mixed | $83,000 |

### Key Takeaways

1. **Margins decrease as you scale** (but profit increases)
2. **Team is the biggest cost** at scale
3. **Multiple revenue streams** de-risk the business
4. **Retention is more important than acquisition**
5. **Premium offerings** dramatically increase ARPU
