## Recommendations by Scenario

### Scenario 1: Course Creator with Email List

**Situation**: 10k email list, launching first paid community with course content
**Recommendation**: **Skool Pro**
**Why**: Simple setup, course features, gamification, low fees, get started fast

### Scenario 2: SaaS Company Customer Community

**Situation**: Software company wanting customer community for support and retention
**Recommendation**: **Circle Business**
**Why**: Professional appearance, workflows, SSO capability, integrations

### Scenario 3: Gaming Content Creator

**Situation**: YouTube gaming creator wanting fan community
**Recommendation**: **Discord**
**Why**: Gaming audience expects Discord, voice features, free platform

### Scenario 4: Business Coach High-Ticket Mastermind

**Situation**: Coach charging $2,000/month for mastermind community
**Recommendation**: **Circle Business** or **Mighty Networks Growth**
**Why**: Professional appearance, lower transaction fees at high prices

### Scenario 5: Starting from Zero

**Situation**: No audience, want to build community from scratch
**Recommendation**: **Facebook Groups** → **Skool**
**Why**: Start free on FB to build email list, migrate to Skool when ready to monetize

### Scenario 6: Enterprise Training Community

**Situation**: Large company wanting internal learning community
**Recommendation**: **Circle Plus** or **Mighty Networks Pro**
**Why**: SSO, security features, white-label options

## Final Recommendation

### If You're Unsure, Start Here

**For most creators building paid communities**: **Skool Pro**
- Lowest barrier to entry
- All-in-one solution
- Reasonable fees
- Can always migrate later

**For professional/B2B communities**: **Circle Professional**
- More polished
- Better integrations
- Workflow automation available
- Grows with you

**For testing/validation**: **Discord** (free) or **Facebook Groups** (free)
- No cost to start
- Validate demand
- Build email list
- Migrate when ready

The best platform is the one where your audience will engage. When in doubt, ask them.
