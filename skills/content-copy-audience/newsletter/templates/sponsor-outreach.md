# Sponsor Outreach Templates: Complete Email Collection

These templates are battle-tested for sponsor acquisition. Customize for your newsletter and audience.

---

## Cold Outreach Templates

### Template 1: The Direct Approach

**Subject:** Sponsoring [Newsletter Name] - [Audience Type] audience

```
Hi [First Name],

I run [Newsletter Name], a [frequency] newsletter reaching [number] [audience type].

Our readers include [specific audience details] who are actively interested in [topics].

Key stats:
• [Subscriber count] subscribers
• [Open rate]% average open rate
• [Click rate]% average click rate

I think [Company] would be a great fit for our audience because [specific reason].

Would you be interested in sponsoring an issue? I'd be happy to send our media kit with more details.

Best,
[Your name]
[Newsletter name]
[Link]
```

**When to use:** General outreach to qualified prospects

---

### Template 2: The Observation Opener

**Subject:** Saw [Company] is advertising in [Similar Newsletter]

```
Hi [First Name],

I noticed [Company] recently sponsored [Similar Newsletter] (or ran ads on [Platform]).

I run [Newsletter Name], which reaches a similar audience of [audience description] - [number] subscribers with [open rate]% open rates.

Our content focuses on [topics], which seems aligned with what [Company] does.

A few recent sponsors include: [Sponsor 1], [Sponsor 2], [Sponsor 3].

Would you be open to exploring a sponsorship? Happy to share more details.

Best,
[Your name]
```

**When to use:** When you've seen them advertise elsewhere

---

### Template 3: The Product User

**Subject:** Longtime [Product] user interested in partnership

```
Hi [First Name],

I've been using [Product] for [time period] and genuinely love it for [specific reason].

I also run [Newsletter Name], a newsletter for [audience description] with [subscribers] subscribers.

I've mentioned [Product] organically a few times, and readers always respond well.

I'd love to explore a formal sponsorship. This would let me share [Product] with our full audience in a dedicated way.

Interested in discussing?

Best,
[Your name]
[Newsletter name]
```

**When to use:** When you're a genuine user of their product

---

### Template 4: The Value-First

**Subject:** Quick idea for reaching [Audience Type]

```
Hi [First Name],

I've been following [Company]'s growth and have an idea that might help reach more [target audience].

I publish [Newsletter Name] to [number] [audience type]. Every [frequency], they read our take on [topics].

Our audience fits [Company]'s target profile because [specific reasons].

A recent sponsor, [Company Name], saw [specific result] from their campaign.

I'd love to share more about how we might work together. Worth a quick call?

Best,
[Your name]
```

**When to use:** When you have social proof/results to share

---

### Template 5: The Referral

**Subject:** [Mutual Connection] suggested I reach out

```
Hi [First Name],

[Mutual Connection] mentioned you might be interested in reaching [audience type] for [Company].

I run [Newsletter Name], which goes to [number] [audience description] every [frequency].

[Mutual Connection] has sponsored before and can speak to the experience. (Or: [Mutual Connection] thought our audiences would align well.)

Would you be open to a quick chat about sponsorship opportunities?

Best,
[Your name]

P.S. Happy to send our media kit if you'd like to see more details first.
```

**When to use:** When you have a referral or mutual connection

## Follow-Up Templates

```
Quick reminder: We reach [number] [audience type] with [open rate]% open rates.

I'd be happy to jump on a quick call or send more information.

Best,
[Your name]
```

## Objection Handling Templates

### "Too Expensive"

```
Hi [First Name],

I understand budget is a consideration.

A few options:

1. Try a smaller placement first - our classified spots are $[price] and let you test the audience.

2. Package deal - if you book [3+] issues, I can offer [X]% off.

3. Flexible timing - we sometimes have last-minute availability at reduced rates.

Would any of these work for your budget?

Best,
[Your name]
```

---

### "Not the Right Time"

```
Hi [First Name],

Totally understand - timing is everything.

When would be a better time for [Company]? A few common timelines:
• Q1 planning (reach out in December)
• Product launch
• Campaign season

I'll make a note and reach back out then. Or feel free to email me when you're ready.

Thanks for letting me know.

Best,
[Your name]
```

---

### "Need to Think About It"

```
Hi [First Name],

Absolutely - take your time.

To help with the decision, here are a few things other sponsors found useful:
• [Link to case study or testimonial]
• [Link to sample issue]
• [Link to media kit]

Happy to answer any specific questions as you consider.

Best,
[Your name]
```

## Creative Request Templates

### Initial Creative Request

**Subject:** [Newsletter Name] x [Company] - Creative needed by [Date]

```
Hi [First Name],

Excited to have [Company] in [Newsletter Name] on [Date]!

Here's what we need by [Deadline]:

COPY:
• Headline: Up to [X] words
• Body: [X] words max
• CTA: Button text

OPTIONAL IMAGE:
• Size: [X] x [X] pixels
• Format: PNG or JPG

LINKS:
• Primary URL (with any tracking parameters)

Please include any disclosure language you need.

Questions? Just reply.

Best,
[Your name]
```

```
Best,
[Your name]
```

## Quick Reference: Subject Line Formulas

**Cold Outreach:**
- "Sponsoring [Newsletter Name] - [Audience] audience"
- "Reaching [Number] [Audience Type]"
- "Saw [Company] advertising to [Audience]"
- "Partnership idea for [Company]"

**Follow-Up:**
- "Re: [Previous Subject]"
- "Quick follow-up on [Newsletter Name]"
- "Last one from me"

**Results/Rebooking:**
- "[Company] x [Newsletter Name] Results"
- "Ready for round 2?"
- "Your campaign results are in"
