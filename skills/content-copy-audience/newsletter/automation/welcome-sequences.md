# Welcome Sequences: 5 Complete Templates

Your welcome sequence is the most important automation you'll create. It sets the tone, builds the relationship, and determines long-term engagement. These 5 templates cover every newsletter type.

---

## Why Welcome Sequences Matter

### The Impact

| With Welcome Sequence | Without |
|----------------------|---------|
| 50%+ open rates | 35% opens |
| 3-5 replies average | Near zero |
| 10%+ referrals | <2% |
| Lower churn | Higher churn |
| Faster monetization | Slower |

### The Window

**First 7 days are critical:**
- Highest engagement period
- Reader forms habits
- Trust established
- Expectations set

### Welcome Sequence Goals

1. **Confirm value** - They made the right choice
2. **Deliver promise** - Give them what they signed up for
3. **Build relationship** - Establish connection
4. **Set expectations** - What to expect going forward
5. **Drive action** - Referral, reply, engagement

## Template 1: The Classic 5-Email Sequence

**Best for:** General newsletters, news/curation, broad audiences

### Email 1: The Warm Welcome (Immediate)

**Subject:** Welcome to [Newsletter] 🎉

```
Hey [Name],

You're in! Welcome to [Newsletter Name].

I'm [Your Name], and I write this newsletter because [brief why statement].

Here's what to expect:
• [Frequency]: You'll hear from me every [day]
• [Content]: Each issue covers [what you cover]
• [Length]: Takes about [X] minutes to read

To make sure you never miss an issue, add [email] to your contacts.

Got a quick second? Reply and tell me: What's your biggest challenge with [topic]?

I read every reply.

Talk soon,
[Your name]

P.S. Here are my 3 most popular issues if you want to dive in:
• [Link to popular post 1]
• [Link to popular post 2]
• [Link to popular post 3]
```

**Timing:** Immediate on signup

## Sequence Implementation Checklist

### Setup
- [ ] Choose template that fits your newsletter
- [ ] Customize copy for your voice and content
- [ ] Set up automation in your platform
- [ ] Test all links and delivery
- [ ] Review on mobile

### Timing
- [ ] Email 1: Immediate
- [ ] Email 2: 24 hours
- [ ] Email 3: 48 hours
- [ ] Email 4: 4 days
- [ ] Email 5: 7 days

### Monitoring
- [ ] Track open rates per email
- [ ] Monitor replies
- [ ] Check click-through rates
- [ ] A/B test subject lines
- [ ] Iterate based on data

### Optimization
- [ ] Review monthly
- [ ] Update based on feedback
- [ ] Test new approaches
- [ ] Refresh quarterly
