# Newsletter Empire

Master newsletter growth, monetization, and operations to scale to 250k+ subscribers.

## What It Does

This skill provides comprehensive newsletter operations knowledge—from platform selection and growth strategies to sponsorship management and revenue optimization. Build and monetize a thriving newsletter business.

## Features

- Newsletter platform comparisons (Beehiiv, ConvertKit, Substack, Ghost)
- Growth levers (referrals, paid acquisition, cross-promotions)
- Lead magnet and landing page optimization
- Deliverability and list hygiene management
- Sponsorship pricing and outreach
- Paid subscription strategies
- Content planning and calendar management
- Automation and welcome sequences

## Installation

1. Copy the `newsletter/` folder into your OpenClaw skills directory
2. Restart OpenClaw

## Usage

Use this skill when:
- Selecting a newsletter platform
- Planning growth campaigns
- Pricing sponsorships
- Writing newsletter content
- Optimizing deliverability
- Setting up referral programs
- Creating automated sequences

## Requirements

- Newsletter platform account
- Email service provider
- SparkLoop or Beehiiv for referrals (optional)
- Landing page builder
- Sponsor outreach tools
