# Beehiiv: Complete Platform Guide

Beehiiv has emerged as the leading newsletter platform for growth-focused operators. Built by former Morning Brew team members, it combines sophisticated growth tools with a clean creator experience. This guide covers everything you need to know.

---

## Platform Overview

### What Beehiiv Is

Beehiiv is an all-in-one newsletter platform designed for:
- Newsletter creators prioritizing growth
- Operators who want built-in monetization tools
- Publishers looking for modern, scalable infrastructure
- Teams needing collaboration features

### History and Positioning

Founded in 2021 by Morning Brew alumni Tyler Denk and Benjamin Hargett, Beehiiv was built to democratize the growth tactics that powered Morning Brew's success. The platform launched publicly in 2022 and has grown rapidly, now hosting thousands of newsletters including some of the fastest-growing publications.

**Key Philosophy:**
- Growth tools built-in, not bolted-on
- Creator-first economics (0% on paid subs on paid plans)
- Modern, clean user experience
- Data transparency and ownership

## Growth Features

### Recommendation Network

Beehiiv's built-in cross-promotion system.

**How It Works:**
1. You recommend other newsletters in the network
2. Other newsletters recommend you
3. Shown on thank-you page after subscription
4. Reciprocal relationship building

**Best Practices:**
- Recommend newsletters your audience actually likes
- Choose complementary, not competing newsletters
- Rotate recommendations periodically
- Track which recs drive your growth

**Typical Results:**
- 5-15% of new subscribers come from recommendations
- Quality varies by partner
- No cost (unless using Boosts)

### Boosts Network

Beehiiv's paid subscriber acquisition marketplace.

**How It Works:**
1. Publishers offer to promote your newsletter
2. You pay per subscriber acquired
3. Pricing set by publishers
4. Quality verified by Beehiiv

**Typical Pricing:**
| Newsletter Type | Cost per Subscriber |
|----------------|---------------------|
| General/broad audience | $1.00-2.00 |
| Business/professional | $2.00-4.00 |
| Niche/high-value | $3.00-6.00 |

**Boost as a Publisher:**
- Earn money promoting other newsletters
- Set your own CPS price
- Choose which newsletters to promote
- Passive revenue from your audience

**Best Practices:**
- Start with small budget to test quality
- Track 30-day retention of boosted subscribers
- Calculate effective CPA including churn
- Quality > quantity in partner selection

### Referral Program

Built-in viral loop mechanism.

**How It Works:**
1. Subscriber gets unique referral link
2. They share with friends
3. Track referrals per subscriber
4. Reward milestones with prizes

**Reward Tier Examples:**
| Referrals | Reward |
|-----------|--------|
| 1 | Unlock bonus content |
| 3 | Digital download |
| 5 | Exclusive access |
| 10 | Physical merchandise |
| 25 | Premium tier or course |
| 50+ | Major reward (1:1 call, etc.) |

**Referral Widget:**
Embed directly in your newsletter—shows:
- Current referral count
- Next milestone
- Share buttons
- Referral link

**Typical Performance:**
- 5-15% of subscribers will refer at least 1 person
- 1-3% will become "super referrers" (5+ referrals)
- 10-30% of monthly growth from referrals (when optimized)

### Magic Links

Passwordless authentication for paid subscribers.

**How It Works:**
1. Subscriber clicks link in email
2. Automatically authenticated on website
3. No password needed
4. Access to premium content

**Benefits:**
- Zero friction for paid subscribers
- Higher engagement with web content
- No password reset support
- Seamless cross-device access

## Monetization Features

### Ad Network

Beehiiv's built-in sponsorship marketplace.

**How It Works:**
1. Advertisers submit campaigns
2. Beehiiv matches to relevant newsletters
3. You accept/reject ad opportunities
4. Ads inserted automatically
5. Revenue tracked and paid

**Requirements:**
- Scale plan or above
- Minimum subscriber count (varies)
- Good deliverability metrics
- Active newsletter

**Typical CPMs:**
| Category | CPM Range |
|----------|-----------|
| General | $10-25 |
| Business | $25-50 |
| Tech | $30-60 |
| Finance | $40-80 |

**Pros:**
- Passive income (ads come to you)
- Beehiiv handles billing/collection
- Quality advertisers vetted
- Easy to start

**Cons:**
- Lower CPM than direct sales
- Less control over advertisers
- Revenue share with Beehiiv
- Not available in all regions

### Paid Subscriptions

**Features:**
- Monthly or annual billing
- Multiple price tiers
- Free trials
- Discount codes
- Subscriber management
- Payment processing (Stripe)

**Economics:**
- 0% take rate on Scale and Max plans
- You pay Stripe processing (~2.9% + 30¢)
- Full revenue minus processing to you

**Paid Content Options:**
- Entire posts paywalled
- Specific sections paywalled
- Early access for paid subscribers
- Exclusive posts for paid only
- Full archive access

### Digital Products

Sell digital products directly through Beehiiv.

**Product Types:**
- E-books and PDFs
- Digital downloads
- Course access
- Membership/community access
- Appointment scheduling (Max plan)

**Features:**
- One-time payments
- Subscription payments
- Built-in delivery
- Purchase history
- Revenue tracking

### Sponsorship Storefront (Max Plan)

**Features:**
- Public page for sponsor inquiries
- Pricing display
- Availability calendar
- Direct booking requests
- Self-serve sponsor management

## Migration to Beehiiv

### From Mailchimp/ConvertKit/etc.

1. Export subscriber list (CSV)
2. Create Beehiiv account
3. Import subscribers with field mapping
4. Recreate templates and automations
5. Set up custom domain
6. Update subscription forms
7. Redirect old links

### From Substack

1. Export subscribers from Substack
2. Export post archive
3. Import to Beehiiv
4. Set up redirects for SEO
5. Notify audience of move
6. Handle paid subscriber migration (Stripe)

### Migration Support

Beehiiv offers migration assistance:
- Free for Scale and Max plans
- Help with subscriber import
- Template recreation assistance
- Technical setup support

## Beehiiv Tips and Best Practices

### Quick Wins

1. **Enable referral program immediately** - Even basic rewards drive growth
2. **Join recommendation network** - Free subscriber source
3. **Use A/B testing on every send** - Subject lines matter
4. **Set up custom domain** - Professionalism and deliverability
5. **Add referral widget to emails** - Passive referral reminders

### Advanced Tactics

1. **Segment by engagement** - Re-engage cold subscribers
2. **Use Boosts strategically** - Test quality before scaling
3. **Create welcome automation** - First impressions matter
4. **Track source quality** - Not all subscribers equal
5. **Leverage 3D analytics** - Find churn patterns

### Common Mistakes

1. **Not using growth features** - They're built-in for a reason
2. **Ignoring deliverability** - Clean list regularly
3. **Under-pricing sponsorships** - Know your value
4. **Not A/B testing** - Easy optimization left on table
5. **Skipping welcome sequence** - Critical for engagement
