## Use Case Recommendations

### "I want to grow as fast as possible"
**Choose: Beehiiv**
- Built-in referral program
- Boosts paid acquisition
- Recommendation network
- Flat pricing enables investment

### "I sell courses and digital products"
**Choose: ConvertKit**
- Commerce built-in
- Advanced automation for funnels
- Product delivery systems
- Sequence-based sales

### "I'm a writer who wants paid subscribers"
**Choose: Substack**
- Network discovery
- Simple paid setup
- Writer community
- Zero learning curve

### "I want complete control and customization"
**Choose: Ghost**
- Open source
- Full theme control
- 0% take rate
- True ownership

### "I'm monetizing through sponsors"
**Choose: Beehiiv**
- Built-in ad network
- Sponsor storefront
- Best sponsor tools
- Flat pricing regardless of size

### "I have complex subscriber journeys"
**Choose: ConvertKit**
- Visual automation builder
- Advanced segmentation
- Tag-based workflows
- Behavior triggers

### "I'm just starting and want free"
**Choose: Beehiiv or Substack**
- Both have generous free tiers
- Beehiiv: 2.5k subs free + growth tools
- Substack: Unlimited free + network

### "I'm building a media brand"
**Choose: Ghost**
- Full website capabilities
- Multiple authors
- SEO optimization
- Professional appearance

## Decision Framework

### Step 1: Define Your Primary Goal

| Goal | Best Platform |
|------|---------------|
| Maximum growth | Beehiiv |
| Paid subscriptions | Substack or Ghost |
| Selling products | ConvertKit |
| Full customization | Ghost |
| Sponsor revenue | Beehiiv |
| Complex automation | ConvertKit |

### Step 2: Consider Your Constraints

| Constraint | Best Platform |
|------------|---------------|
| Zero budget | Substack or Beehiiv free |
| Non-technical | Substack |
| At scale (100k+) | Beehiiv or Ghost |
| Need website | Ghost |
| Need simplicity | Substack |

### Step 3: Evaluate Trade-offs

**Beehiiv Trade-offs:**
- ✅ Best growth tools, flat pricing
- ❌ Less automation than Kit

**ConvertKit Trade-offs:**
- ✅ Best automation, commerce
- ❌ Expensive at scale

**Substack Trade-offs:**
- ✅ Network effects, simplicity
- ❌ 10% take rate, limited features

**Ghost Trade-offs:**
- ✅ Full control, 0% take
- ❌ Technical complexity
