## Before/After Examples

### Weak → Strong Transformations

| Weak Title | Strong Title |
|------------|--------------|
| How to Get Views | How to Get Your First 10,000 Views (Even With 0 Subscribers) |
| Best Productivity Tips | 7 Productivity Hacks That Saved Me 20 Hours/Week |
| Making Money Online | How I Made $5,000 This Month (Working 2 Hours/Day) |
| Learn to Code | Learn Python in 30 Days (Complete Beginner's Guide) |
| Get Fit at Home | Build Muscle Without Equipment (15-Min Daily Routine) |
| Save Money | How I Saved $10,000 in 6 Months (On a $40K Salary) |
| Better Sleep | How to Fall Asleep in Under 5 Minutes (Works Every Time) |
| Healthy Eating | Meal Prep 5 Days of Food in 2 Hours (Under $50) |

### What Makes Them Better

1. **Specific numbers** instead of vague promises
2. **Timeframes** that set expectations
3. **Objection handling** in parentheses
4. **Results-focused** language
5. **Curiosity elements** that demand clicks

## A/B Testing Strategies

### What to Test

**Element variations**:
- Different numbers (5 vs 7 vs 10)
- With/without parenthetical
- Question vs statement
- Specific vs general
- Power words vs plain language

### Testing Pairs to Try

**Pair 1: Number Variation**
- A: "7 Ways to Improve Your Sleep"
- B: "5 Simple Ways to Improve Your Sleep Tonight"

**Pair 2: Promise Specificity**
- A: "How to Make Money Online"
- B: "How I Made $3,000 This Month Working from Home"

**Pair 3: Format Style**
- A: "The Complete Guide to [Topic]"
- B: "[Topic] 101: Everything You Need to Know"

**Pair 4: Curiosity Angle**
- A: "10 Mistakes Beginners Make"
- B: "10 Mistakes Beginners Make (Number 7 Surprised Me)"

### Interpreting Results

- **>2% CTR difference**: Statistically significant
- **Same CTR but different AVD**: Content match issue
- **Higher CTR but lower AVD**: Title overpromised

## Title Length Optimization

### Character Counts

**Optimal length**: 50-60 characters
**Maximum before truncation**: ~70 characters (varies by device)
**Minimum effective**: 30 characters

### Truncation Points

**Desktop**: ~70 characters
**Mobile**: ~50-60 characters
**Search results**: ~60 characters
**Suggested videos**: ~50 characters

### Priority Placement

Put the most important information in the first 50 characters:

**Good**: "How I Made $10K/Month with AI (Step-by-Step)"
- Core promise visible even when truncated

**Bad**: "The Complete Beginner's Guide to How I Made $10K/Month with AI"
- Key information buried at the end

### Length Examples

| Characters | Example |
|------------|---------|
| 30 | "5 Sleep Hacks That Work" |
| 45 | "5 Sleep Hacks That Actually Work (Tested)" |
| 60 | "5 Science-Backed Sleep Hacks That Actually Work (I Tested All)" |
| 70+ | "5 Science-Backed Sleep Hacks That Actually Work (I Tested Them All For 30 Days)" |
