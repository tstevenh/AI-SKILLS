# Metrics That Matter

You can't improve what you don't measure. This chapter covers the essential metrics for cold email and what benchmarks to target.

## Primary Metrics

### Open Rate

**Definition**: Percentage of delivered emails that were opened.

**Formula**: (Unique Opens / Delivered Emails) × 100

**Benchmarks by Industry**:
| Industry | Poor | Average | Good | Great |
|----------|------|---------|------|-------|
| Technology | <30% | 35-45% | 45-55% | 55%+ |
| Professional Services | <35% | 40-50% | 50-60% | 60%+ |
| Finance | <25% | 30-40% | 40-50% | 50%+ |
| Healthcare | <30% | 35-45% | 45-55% | 55%+ |

**What It Tells You**: Subject line effectiveness + sender reputation + list quality

**Caveats**:
- Apple Mail Privacy Protection inflates open rates
- Opens are tracked via pixel, which can be blocked
- Use as directional, not absolute measure

### Reply Rate

**Definition**: Percentage of delivered emails that received a reply.

**Formula**: (Total Replies / Delivered Emails) × 100

**Benchmarks**:
| Category | Poor | Average | Good | Great | Elite |
|----------|------|---------|------|-------|-------|
| Cold Email | <2% | 3-5% | 5-10% | 10-15% | 15%+ |

**What It Tells You**: Overall email effectiveness (subject + body + CTA + targeting)

**Why It's Important**: The most reliable metric—actually measures engagement

### Positive Reply Rate

**Definition**: Percentage of replies that express interest.

**Formula**: (Positive Replies / Delivered Emails) × 100

**Benchmarks**:
| Category | Poor | Average | Good | Great |
|----------|------|---------|------|-------|
| Cold Email | <1% | 1-2% | 2-5% | 5%+ |

**What It Tells You**: True effectiveness—are you reaching people who want what you offer?

**Categorizing Replies**:
- **Positive**: Interested, wants more info, books meeting
- **Neutral**: Referral, timing question, needs more context
- **Negative**: Not interested, unsubscribe request, harsh rejection

### Meeting Booked Rate

**Definition**: Percentage of positive replies that convert to meetings.

**Formula**: (Meetings Booked / Positive Replies) × 100

**Benchmarks**:
| Category | Poor | Average | Good | Great |
|----------|------|---------|------|-------|
| Meeting Conversion | <30% | 35-45% | 45-60% | 60%+ |

**What It Tells You**: Follow-up effectiveness and qualification accuracy

## Secondary Metrics

### Bounce Rate

**Definition**: Percentage of sent emails that failed to deliver.

**Formula**: (Bounced Emails / Sent Emails) × 100

**Types**:
- **Hard Bounce**: Permanent failure (invalid address)
- **Soft Bounce**: Temporary failure (mailbox full, server issue)

**Benchmarks**:
| Status | Rate |
|--------|------|
| Excellent | <1% |
| Good | 1-2% |
| Concerning | 2-5% |
| Critical | >5% |

**What It Tells You**: List quality and data hygiene

### Spam Complaint Rate

**Definition**: Percentage of recipients who marked email as spam.

**Formula**: (Spam Complaints / Delivered Emails) × 100

**Benchmarks**:
| Status | Rate |
|--------|------|
| Safe | <0.1% |
| Monitor | 0.1-0.3% |
| Warning | 0.3-0.5% |
| Danger | >0.5% |

**What It Tells You**: Relevance, list quality, sender reputation risk

### Unsubscribe Rate

**Definition**: Percentage of recipients who unsubscribed.

**Formula**: (Unsubscribes / Delivered Emails) × 100

**Benchmarks**: <1% is acceptable for cold email

**What It Tells You**: Relevance and list quality

## Funnel Metrics

### The Cold Email Funnel

```
Emails Sent (100%)
    ↓
Emails Delivered (98-99%)
    ↓
Emails Opened (45-55%)
    ↓
Emails Read (portion of opens)
    ↓
Replies (5-10%)
    ↓
Positive Replies (2-5%)
    ↓
Meetings Booked (1-3%)
    ↓
Opportunities (0.5-1.5%)
    ↓
Closed Deals (0.1-0.5%)
```

### Calculating Pipeline Impact

**Example Funnel**:
- 1,000 emails sent
- 980 delivered (2% bounce)
- 490 opened (50% open rate)
- 49 replies (10% reply rate of opens)
- 25 positive (50% positive of replies)
- 15 meetings (60% book rate)
- 8 opportunities (50% qualify rate)
- 2 deals (25% win rate)

If average deal = $10,000, then:
- Revenue per 1,000 emails: $20,000
- Cost per email: ~$0.50 (tools, time)
- Total cost: $500
- ROI: 40x

### Benchmarks by Stage

| Stage | Conversion Rate |
|-------|-----------------|
| Sent → Delivered | 97-99% |
| Delivered → Opened | 40-55% |
| Opened → Replied | 8-15% |
| Replied → Positive | 40-60% |
| Positive → Meeting | 50-70% |
| Meeting → Opportunity | 40-60% |
| Opportunity → Deal | 20-40% |

## Tracking and Reporting

### Daily Tracking
- Emails sent/delivered
- Bounces (investigate immediately)
- Opens/replies (directional check)
- Any spam complaints

### Weekly Analysis
- Reply rate by campaign
- Reply rate by day of week
- Positive reply rate
- Open rates by subject line
- Sequence step performance

### Monthly Review
- Full funnel analysis
- Cost per meeting
- Cost per opportunity
- Pipeline generated
- Revenue attribution
- Trend analysis

## Summary: Metrics Principles

**Reply Rate is King**: The most reliable and meaningful metric.

**Track the Full Funnel**: Don't just measure opens—measure revenue.

**Benchmark Appropriately**: Compare to your industry and use case.

**Trend Over Absolutes**: Are you improving over time?

**Quality Over Quantity**: High reply rates beat high volume.
