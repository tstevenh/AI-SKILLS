## Usage Notes

### Customization Required
These templates are starting points. For best results:
- Research the recipient thoroughly
- Customize every bracketed element
- Add genuine personalization beyond templates
- Match tone to your brand and audience

### Testing
- A/B test different templates
- Track which approaches work for your audience
- Iterate based on results

### Avoiding Template Detection
Recipients can often spot templates. To prevent this:
- Vary your templates
- Add genuine personal touches
- Reference specific, recent information
- Sound like a human, not a robot

---

*Use these templates as frameworks, not copy-paste solutions. The best emails are personalized to each recipient.*
