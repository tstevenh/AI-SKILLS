## Usage Guidelines

### Customization Steps
1. **Industry Research**: Understand current trends and challenges
2. **Company Research**: Find specific observations about them
3. **Similar Customer**: Use the most relevant case study
4. **Specific Metrics**: Use real numbers from your results
5. **Relevant CTA**: Match ask to their likely buying process

### Industry-Specific Considerations

| Industry | Key Concerns | Typical Buyers | Sales Cycle |
|----------|--------------|----------------|-------------|
| SaaS | ROI, integration | IT, Ops, C-suite | 1-3 months |
| Finance | Compliance, security | IT, Compliance, C-suite | 3-12 months |
| Healthcare | HIPAA, patient outcomes | Clinical, IT, Admin | 6-18 months |
| Retail | Revenue, customer exp | Marketing, Ops, C-suite | 1-6 months |
| Manufacturing | Efficiency, quality | Ops, Engineering, C-suite | 3-12 months |
