# Platform Analytics Walkthrough: Using Native Analytics Tools

## Overview

Each short-form platform provides native analytics that reveal how your content performs. Understanding these tools is essential for data-driven optimization. This guide walks through each platform's analytics interface and explains how to extract actionable insights.

---

## TikTok Analytics

### Accessing TikTok Analytics

**Requirements:**
- Account must be at least 7 days old
- Need Business or Creator account type

**How to Access:**
1. Open TikTok app
2. Tap Profile
3. Tap three-line menu (☰)
4. Tap Creator Tools
5. Tap Analytics

### Overview Tab

**What You See:**
- Video views (7/28/60 days)
- Profile views
- Likes
- Comments
- Shares
- Followers

**Key Insights:**
- Overall engagement trend (up/down)
- Which time periods performed better
- Growth trajectory

### Content Tab

**Video Performance:**
- Individual video metrics
- Views, likes, comments, shares for each
- Average watch time
- Watch full video %
- Traffic source breakdown

**Trending Videos:**
Top 9 performing videos in selected period.

**Key Insights:**
- Which content types perform best
- What hooks work (from top performers)
- Where traffic comes from

### Followers Tab

**Demographics:**
- Gender split
- Top territories (countries)
- Follower activity times

**Follower Growth:**
- Net follower change
- Growth over time

**Key Insights:**
- When to post (activity times)
- Who your audience is
- Whether you're reaching target demographic

### LIVE Tab (If Applicable)

**LIVE Metrics:**
- Total views
- New followers from LIVE
- Top viewers
- Diamonds received

### Using TikTok Analytics Effectively

**Daily Check (2 minutes):**
- View count on recent videos
- Any standout performers

**Weekly Review (15 minutes):**
- Compare this week to last
- Identify top 3 performers
- Note follower activity times
- Check for patterns

**Monthly Deep Dive (30 minutes):**
- Full content audit
- Demographic review
- Growth trajectory analysis
- Strategy adjustment

### TikTok Analytics Limitations

**What's Missing:**
- Save count on individual videos
- Detailed demographic breakdown
- Audience retention graph
- Click-through rate

**Workarounds:**
- Note saves from notification count
- Use third-party analytics tools
- Manual tracking for deeper insights

## YouTube Analytics for Shorts

### Accessing YouTube Analytics

**How to Access:**
1. Open YouTube Studio (app or desktop)
2. Click Analytics in left menu
3. Navigate to Content tab
4. Filter by "Shorts"

### Overview Tab

**Key Metrics:**
- Views
- Watch time
- Subscribers
- Revenue (if monetized)

**Multiple Time Ranges:**
- Last 7 days
- Last 28 days
- Last 90 days
- Custom range

### Content Tab (Filtered for Shorts)

**Individual Short Metrics:**
- Views
- Impressions
- Click-through rate (for Shorts in search/browse)
- Average view duration
- Likes, comments

**Key Metrics for Shorts:**
- Shorts views
- Shorts watch time
- Performance vs. long-form

### Audience Tab

**Who's Watching:**
- Returning vs. new viewers
- Unique viewers
- Subscribers vs. non-subscribers
- Age and gender breakdown
- Geography
- When viewers are on YouTube

**Important for Shorts:**
- What % of Shorts viewers subscribe
- Do Shorts viewers watch long-form?
- Audience overlap between formats

### Reach Tab

**How Viewers Find You:**
- Browse features
- Shorts feed
- Channel pages
- Search
- External sources

**Shorts-Specific Traffic:**
- Shorts feed views
- Swipe vs. direct views

### Revenue Tab (If Monetized)

**Shorts Revenue:**
- Ad revenue share
- Super Thanks
- Channel memberships

**Note:** Shorts revenue shows separately from long-form.

### Using YouTube Analytics for Shorts

**Key Questions to Answer:**

1. **Which Shorts drive subscribers?**
Navigate to: Content > Individual Short > Subscribers
This tells you which content converts viewers to followers.

2. **Do Shorts viewers watch long-form?**
Check: Audience > Returning viewers patterns
Compare Shorts audience to long-form audience.

3. **What's my Shorts-specific performance?**
Filter Content tab by Shorts to isolate performance.

### YouTube Analytics Unique Features

**Retention Curve:**
YouTube shows when viewers drop off. For Shorts:
- Sharp early drop = hook problem
- Steady decline = normal
- Rewatch spike = content loops well

**Subscriber Source:**
See exactly which videos/Shorts drive subscriptions.

**Real-Time Analytics:**
See how new content performs in first 48 hours.

## Instagram Insights for Reels

### Accessing Instagram Insights

**Requirements:**
- Business or Creator account
- At least 100 followers (some features)

**How to Access:**

**Profile Level:**
1. Go to Profile
2. Tap Professional Dashboard
3. Tap "See all" under Account Insights

**Individual Reel:**
1. Open the Reel
2. Tap three dots (...)
3. Tap "View insights"

### Overview Insights

**Accounts Reached:**
- Followers vs. Non-followers
- Content interactions
- Profile activity
- How reach is trending

**Accounts Engaged:**
- Followers vs. Non-followers
- Interactions by type (likes, comments, shares, saves)

### Content Insights (Reels)

**For Each Reel:**
- Plays
- Accounts reached
- Likes
- Comments
- Saves
- Shares
- Profile visits
- Follows

**Reach Breakdown:**
- From Explore
- From Hashtags
- From Reels feed
- From Profile
- From Other

### Audience Insights

**Follower Demographics:**
- Age ranges
- Gender split
- Top locations (cities and countries)
- Most active times

**Follower Growth:**
- Followers gained/lost
- Growth over time

### Using Instagram Insights Effectively

**Key Metrics for Reels:**

**1. Reach from Non-Followers**
This tells you if Reels are driving discovery.
High non-follower reach = content resonating beyond current audience.

**2. Saves and Shares**
The highest-value engagements.
High saves = valuable content worth keeping.
High shares = content worth recommending.

**3. Profile Visits and Follows**
Conversion metrics.
High profile visits = interest in more.
High follows = successful conversion.

### Instagram Insights Limitations

**What's Missing:**
- Watch time/retention data
- Detailed view timing
- Audience retention curve
- Real-time analytics

**Workarounds:**
- Use Creator Studio on desktop for more data
- Third-party tools (Later, Sprout, etc.)
- Manual tracking and testing

- Just starting out
- Single platform focus
- Budget constrained
- Native meets needs

## Building Your Analytics System

### The Minimum Viable System

**Weekly Tracking (Spreadsheet):**

Track for each video:
- Platform
- Post date
- Content type
- Topic
- Hook used
- Views (7-day)
- Engagement rate
- Saves/Shares
- Follower change

### The Comprehensive System

**Daily:**
- Quick check on new video performance
- Note standout results

**Weekly:**
- Update tracking spreadsheet
- Compare to previous week
- Identify patterns

**Monthly:**
- Full content audit
- Platform comparison
- Strategy adjustment
- Goal setting

### What to Do With Data

**The Analysis → Action Loop:**

1. **Observe:** What does the data show?
2. **Hypothesize:** Why might this be happening?
3. **Test:** Try something based on hypothesis
4. **Measure:** Did it work?
5. **Repeat:** Continuous improvement

**Example:**
- Observe: Videos with question hooks get 2x views
- Hypothesize: Questions create curiosity
- Test: Use question hooks for 10 videos
- Measure: Compare performance
- Repeat: If working, do more; if not, try different questions

## Analytics Mistakes to Avoid

### Mistake 1: Checking Too Often

**Problem:**
Obsessive checking leads to anxiety and overreaction to normal fluctuations.

**Solution:**
Set specific check times. Once after posting, once next day, weekly review.

### Mistake 2: Vanity Metric Focus

**Problem:**
Celebrating high views without checking engagement or conversion.

**Solution:**
Focus on engagement rate, saves/shares, and follower conversion.

### Mistake 3: Single Video Conclusions

**Problem:**
Drawing strategy conclusions from one video's performance.

**Solution:**
Look at patterns across 10+ videos before making changes.

### Mistake 4: Ignoring the Data

**Problem:**
Creating content based on gut feeling without checking what works.

**Solution:**
Let data inform (not dictate) your content strategy.

### Mistake 5: Comparing Across Niches

**Problem:**
Comparing your performance to creators in different niches.

**Solution:**
Compare to your own history and direct competitors.

## Summary: Analytics Mastery

### The Core Principle

Analytics exist to help you improve, not to judge your worth. Use data as a guide, not an obsession.

### The Essential Metrics

**Watch to the end:**
- TikTok: Watch full video %
- YouTube: Average view duration
- Instagram: Plays (proxy)

**Engagement quality:**
- Saves (high value)
- Shares (highest value)
- Comments (community indicator)

**Conversion:**
- Followers from content
- Profile visits

### The Action Framework

1. Know your benchmarks (your averages)
2. Identify outliers (above and below)
3. Understand why (analyze patterns)
4. Apply learnings (test hypotheses)
5. Repeat continuously

Analytics mastery isn't about complex dashboards—it's about consistently asking "what's working?" and doing more of it.
