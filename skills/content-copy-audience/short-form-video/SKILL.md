# Short-Form Video Mastery Skill

## Overview
This skill provides comprehensive knowledge for creating, optimizing, and monetizing short-form video content across TikTok, YouTube Shorts, Instagram Reels, and other platforms. Designed for creators who want to repurpose long-form content and create native short-form videos that drive growth and revenue.

## When to Use This Skill
- Repurposing YouTube videos into Shorts, Reels, and TikToks
- Creating native short-form content from scratch
- Optimizing videos for each platform's algorithm
- Developing hooks that stop the scroll
- Building a content engine for consistent posting
- Monetizing short-form content
- Analyzing performance and iterating

### The 3-Second Rule
Every video needs a hook in the first 1-3 seconds. Without it, viewers scroll. With it, algorithms reward you.

### Content Mix (80/20 Rule)
- 80% Original content (your unique value)
- 20% Trend participation (discovery fuel)

## Workflow: 1 Video → 20 Shorts

1. **Identify Moments** - Find 10-15 "viral-worthy" segments
2. **AI Clip** - Run through OpusClip (or manual clip)
3. **Add Hooks** - Write platform-specific hooks for each
4. **Edit** - Add captions, effects, music
5. **Batch Schedule** - Spread across platforms over 2 weeks
6. **Analyze** - Track which clips perform, double down

## Hook Formula Quick Reference

### The Question Hook
"Have you ever wondered why [interesting thing]?"

### The Bold Claim Hook
"This [thing] changed my entire [outcome]."

### The Controversy Hook
"Unpopular opinion: [contrarian take]"

### The Story Hook
"I was [situation] when [unexpected thing happened]"

### The List Hook
"3 things I wish I knew about [topic]"

## Algorithm Cheat Sheet

### What ALL platforms reward:
- Watch time / completion rate
- Shares (highest signal)
- Saves (second highest)
- Comments
- Profile visits after watching

### What hurts performance:
- TikTok watermarks on other platforms
- Low-quality/blurry video
- No hook in first 3 seconds
- Irrelevant hashtags
- Posting at dead hours

## Monetization Reality Check

### YouTube Shorts
- ~$0.05-0.07 RPM (per 1000 views)
- 1M views = ~$50-70
- Better for funneling to long-form

### TikTok Creator Rewards
- Videos must be 1+ min, 1000+ views
- Varies wildly by niche and region
- Shop affiliate often more lucrative

### Instagram Reels
- Bonuses vary (invite-only)
- Shopping integration strongest
- Best for brand deals

### Real Money Is In:
1. Brand deals (5-10x platform pay)
2. Affiliate marketing
3. Driving to your products/services
4. Building email list
5. Funneling to high-ticket offers

## Key Principles

1. **Consistency > Virality** - Post daily, expect most to flop, but compound wins
2. **First Second Wins** - Hook or die
3. **Platform Native** - Adapt, don't copy-paste
4. **Data Driven** - Let performance guide strategy
5. **Community First** - Reply, engage, build relationships
6. **Test Everything** - A/B test hooks, formats, times
7. **Quality + Quantity** - Both matter, find your sustainable pace
