# Daily Workflow Checklist

## Morning Routine (15-30 min)

### Research & Inspiration
- [ ] Check trending sounds on TikTok
- [ ] Review Explore/FYP for trending formats
- [ ] Note 2-3 content ideas from morning scroll
- [ ] Check comments on recent posts for content ideas

### Publishing
- [ ] Post scheduled content (or create if not batched)
- [ ] Write platform-optimized caption
- [ ] Add relevant hashtags (3-5 TikTok, 5-10 IG, #Shorts for YT)
- [ ] Cross-post to Stories if applicable (IG)

### Engagement (First 30 min After Posting)
- [ ] Respond to early comments
- [ ] Like comments to boost engagement
- [ ] Reply to any DMs related to content
- [ ] Engage with 5-10 posts in your niche

---

## Midday Check-In (10-15 min)

### Performance Review
- [ ] Check views on morning post
- [ ] Note any engagement patterns
- [ ] Respond to new comments
- [ ] Share to Stories if performing well (IG)

### Community Engagement
- [ ] Reply to 5-10 comments on others' content
- [ ] DM 2-3 creators for networking (optional)
- [ ] Save any inspiring content to swipe file

---

## Evening Routine (30-60 min)

### Content Creation (if not batching)
- [ ] Film 1-3 videos for tomorrow/upcoming
- [ ] Edit if time allows
- [ ] Write scripts/outlines for future content

### Analytics Review
- [ ] Check full day performance on all platforms
- [ ] Note which content type performed best
- [ ] Document any viral or underperforming content

### Planning
- [ ] Confirm tomorrow's content is ready
- [ ] Update content calendar if needed
- [ ] Note any trending topics to address tomorrow

---

## Quick Reference: Platform-Specific

### TikTok Daily
- [ ] Post 1-3 videos
- [ ] Check trending sounds
- [ ] Reply to comments within 1 hour
- [ ] Engage on FYP

### YouTube Shorts Daily
- [ ] Post 1-2 Shorts
- [ ] Check analytics for previous Shorts
- [ ] Respond to comments
- [ ] Link to long-form in comments/description

### Instagram Reels Daily
- [ ] Post 1-2 Reels
- [ ] Share to Stories
- [ ] Reply to comments
- [ ] Engage with followers' content

---

## Time Allocation Guide

| Task | Time | Priority |
|------|------|----------|
| Content creation | 1-2 hrs | Critical |
| Publishing | 15-30 min | Critical |
| Engagement (own content) | 30 min | High |
| Community engagement | 30 min | Medium |
| Analytics review | 15 min | Medium |
| Research/inspiration | 15-30 min | Medium |
| Planning/admin | 15 min | Low |

**Total: 2.5-4 hours/day** (can be reduced with batching)

---

## Batching Alternative

If you batch content creation, daily workflow simplifies:

### Daily (Batched Workflow)
- [ ] Publish pre-scheduled content
- [ ] 30 min engagement on own content
- [ ] 15 min community engagement
- [ ] 15 min analytics and planning

**Total: ~1 hour/day** on non-creation days

### Weekly Batch Day
- [ ] Film 5-10 videos (2-3 hours)
- [ ] Edit all videos (2-3 hours)
- [ ] Schedule all posts (30 min)
- [ ] Plan next week's content (30 min)

**Total: 5-7 hours** one day per week

---

## Daily Don'ts

- ❌ Don't post and ghost (engage after posting)
- ❌ Don't ignore comments for hours
- ❌ Don't scroll endlessly without purpose
- ❌ Don't compare every video to your best
- ❌ Don't skip posting due to perfectionism
- ❌ Don't forget to save trending content for reference

---

## Success Metrics to Track Daily

### Views
- Today's views
- Compare to same time yesterday

### Engagement
- Comments (quantity and quality)
- Saves
- Shares

### Growth
- New followers
- Profile visits

### Trends
- What's working
- What's not working
