# Longform Research

Master the art of deep, investigative research for authoritative content creation.

## What It Does

This skill enables comprehensive long-form research—combining primary and secondary sources, data journalism, statistical analysis, and expert interviewing to create authoritative, evidence-based content.

## Features

- Primary vs secondary research methodologies
- Source evaluation and credibility assessment
- Academic research techniques and database access
- Data journalism and investigative patterns
- Survey design and implementation
- Statistical analysis for content
- Expert interview techniques
- Citation styles and attribution
- White paper and report writing

## Installation

1. Copy the `longform-research/` folder into your OpenClaw skills directory
2. Restart OpenClaw

## Usage

Use this skill when:
- Conducting comprehensive topic research
- Writing white papers or industry reports
- Creating data-driven content
- Evaluating source credibility
- Designing and running surveys
- Interviewing subject matter experts
- Analyzing research data

## Requirements

- Access to academic databases (JSTOR, Google Scholar)
- Research tools (Zotero, Mendeley, or similar)
- Survey platform (Typeform, SurveyMonkey, Qualtrics)
- Statistical software or spreadsheet tools
- Interview recording equipment (if applicable)
