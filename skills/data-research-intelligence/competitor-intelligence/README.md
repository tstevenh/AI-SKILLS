# Competitor Intelligence

Systematic competitive research and market intelligence for strategic advantage.

## What It Does

This skill enables comprehensive competitor analysis—SEO, traffic estimation, technology stack detection, pricing intelligence, content strategy reverse-engineering, and market positioning analysis.

## Features

- SEO competitor and keyword gap analysis
- Traffic estimation (SimilarWeb, SEMrush, Ahrefs methodologies)
- Technology stack detection (BuiltWith, Wappalyzer)
- Pricing intelligence and promotion monitoring
- Content strategy analysis and publication frequency
- Social media competitive analysis
- Ad intelligence (Meta Ad Library, Google Ads Transparency)
- Review monitoring and sentiment analysis
- Job posting and hiring intelligence

## Installation

1. Copy the `competitor-intelligence/` folder into your OpenClaw skills directory
2. Restart OpenClaw

## Usage

Use this skill when:
- Analyzing competitor SEO strategies
- Estimating competitor traffic and market share
- Monitoring competitor pricing and promotions
- Reverse-engineering content strategies
- Tracking competitive advertising
- Identifying market opportunities and gaps

## Requirements

- SEO tools (SEMrush, Ahrefs, or SimilarWeb access)
- BuiltWith or Wappalyzer for tech detection
- Social media monitoring tools
- API access for data sources
- Spreadsheet software for analysis
