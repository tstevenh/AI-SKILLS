# Growth Loops Skill Pack

Design and implement self-reinforcing growth mechanisms that turn customers into acquisition channels.

---

## What This Is

A focused skill pack for sustainable, compounding growth:

- **Referral Programs** - Turn customers into advocates with incentive structures
- **Free Tool Strategy** - Build tools that generate leads and SEO traffic

**Think of it as:** Your growth engineering department in Claude Code. Create loops that compound.

**Related Skills:**

- `marketing/email-sequences` - For referral campaign email flows
- `analytics skill` - For measuring viral coefficient and attribution
- `cro/` - For optimizing referral landing pages and signup flows
- `seo/` - For free tool SEO strategy

## Prerequisites

Growth loops work best when you have:

- [ ] Product-market fit (customers who love your product)
- [ ] Positive unit economics (LTV > CAC)
- [ ] Customer satisfaction data (NPS or similar)
- [ ] Technical capacity for implementation

**Warning:** Referral programs amplify what you already have. If customers don't love your product, referrals won't fix that.

---

## Success Criteria

Your growth loop is working when:

- [ ] Viral coefficient > 0.5 (each customer brings 0.5+ new customers)
- [ ] Referral CAC < paid channel CAC
- [ ] Free tool generates consistent lead flow
- [ ] Attribution tracking is in place
- [ ] Loop is self-sustaining (doesn't require constant intervention)

---

## Anti-Patterns

### Don't:

- Launch referral programs before product-market fit
- Offer incentives that attract wrong customers
- Build free tools with no SEO strategy
- Ignore fraud prevention in referral design
- Skip attribution tracking

### Do:

- Validate product love before amplifying
- Design incentives aligned with LTV
- Plan SEO from day one for free tools
- Build fraud detection into program design
- Measure viral coefficient and iterate
