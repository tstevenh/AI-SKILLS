# Sales Email Sequences

## Introduction

Effective sales sequences combine multiple touchpoints across channels to engage prospects. This comprehensive template collection covers outbound prospecting, inbound follow-up, and deal acceleration sequences for SaaS sales teams.

---

## 1. Outbound Prospecting Sequence

### Day 1: Initial Outreach

**Email:**
```
Subject: [Trigger event] at [Company]

Hi [Name],

[Specific observation about their company/role that shows research]

At [Your Company], we help [similar companies/roles] [achieve specific outcome]. 

For example, [Customer Name] [achieved specific result] after implementing our solution.

Would you be open to a brief conversation to see if we could help [Company] achieve similar results?

Best,
[Your Name]

P.S. [Relevant link or resource]
```

### Day 3: LinkedIn Connection

**LinkedIn Request:**
```
Hi [Name],

I reached out via email about [topic]. Would love to connect here as well.

I share content about [relevant topic] that might be useful for your role at [Company].

[Your Name]
```

### Day 5: Value-Add Email

**Email:**
```
Subject: Re: [Previous subject]

Hi [Name],

Wanted to share something relevant to [their initiative/challenge]:

[Resource/insight/article]

[Brief explanation of why it's relevant to them]

Thought it might be helpful as you work on [specific initiative].

Happy to discuss how other [similar roles/companies] are approaching this.

[Your Name]
```

### Day 7: Phone Call (Voicemail Script)

**Voicemail:**
```
"Hi [Name], this is [Your Name] from [Company]. 

I've sent you a couple of emails about [topic]. We're helping companies like [Similar Company] [achieve outcome].

I'll send you a quick follow-up email. If this sounds relevant, I'd love to hear from you.

My number is [number]. Thanks!"
```

### Day 10: Social Proof Email

**Email:**
```
Subject: How [Similar Company] achieved [Result]

Hi [Name],

[Similar Company] was in a situation like yours—[brief challenge description].

After implementing [Your Product], they achieved:
• [Result 1]
• [Result 2]
• [Result 3]

Would it be valuable to see how they did it?

[Your Name]
```

### Day 14: Different Angle Email

**Email:**
```
Subject: Different approach for [Company]

Hi [Name],

I've tried a few times to connect about [topic]. I may have the wrong angle.

Let me try again: Are you exploring ways to [benefit 1], [benefit 2], or [benefit 3]?

If any of these resonate, I'd love to share how we've helped others.

If not, no worries—just let me know and I'll stop reaching out.

[Your Name]
```

### Day 18: LinkedIn Message

**LinkedIn Message:**
```
Hi [Name],

I've reached out a few times about [topic]. Before I close this out, I wanted to check one more time.

Is [challenge/goal] on your radar at [Company]?

Either way, happy to stay connected here.

[Your Name]
```

### Day 21: Breakup Email

**Email:**
```
Subject: Should I close your file?

Hi [Name],

I haven't heard back, so I'll assume the timing isn't right.

I'm going to close your file for now, but if [challenge] ever becomes a priority, I'm here.

Keep my info handy—I'd love to help when you're ready.

Best,
[Your Name]

P.S. If I'm reaching the wrong person for this, please let me know who I should contact.
```

## 2. Inbound Follow-Up Sequence

### Immediate: Trial Started

**Email (Automated):**
```
Subject: Welcome to [Product]—let's get you started

Hi [Name],

Thanks for starting your [Product] trial! I'm [Your Name], and I'll be your point of contact.

Most successful customers do these three things first:
1. [Action 1] — [Link]
2. [Action 2] — [Link]
3. [Action 3] — [Link]

Questions? Just reply to this email. I read every one.

Also, feel free to book time directly: [Calendar link]

Welcome aboard!
[Your Name]
```

### Day 2: Check-In

**Email:**
```
Subject: How's [Product] going so far?

Hi [Name],

You started your trial a couple days ago. How's it going?

If you've hit any roadblocks or have questions, I'm here to help.

Quick tip: Most users find [Feature] really helpful for [use case]. Here's a quick guide: [Link]

Reply anytime or book time here: [Calendar link]

[Your Name]
```

### Day 5: Feature Education

**Email:**
```
Subject: Did you try [Feature] yet?

Hi [Name],

One feature that trial users love: [Feature].

It helps you [benefit], and here's a 2-minute video showing how it works: [Link]

[Customer Name] used it to [specific result].

Give it a try and let me know what you think!

[Your Name]
```

### Day 7: Value Check

**Email:**
```
Subject: Is [Product] working for you?

Hi [Name],

You're a week into your trial. I wanted to check in and see if [Product] is meeting your needs.

Quick questions:
• What are you trying to accomplish?
• Is anything confusing or missing?
• Would a quick walkthrough help?

I want to make sure you get value from your trial. If there's something not working, I'd rather know now so we can fix it.

[Calendar link] if a call would help.

[Your Name]
```

### Day 10: Social Proof

**Email:**
```
Subject: How [Similar Company] uses [Product]

Hi [Name],

Thought you might find this interesting:

[Similar Company] uses [Product] to [specific use case]. They've achieved [specific result].

You can read the full case study here: [Link]

Seeing any similar opportunities at [Company]?

[Your Name]
```

### Day 12: Trial Ending

**Email:**
```
Subject: Your trial ends in 2 days

Hi [Name],

Quick heads up: Your [Product] trial ends on [Date].

Here's what happens:
• Your data stays safe
• You'll move to our free tier
• Upgrade anytime to keep full access

Ready to continue?
→ [Upgrade link]

Want to talk first? [Calendar link]

[Your Name]
```

### Day 14: Trial Expired

**Email:**
```
Subject: Your trial ended—but your data is safe

Hi [Name],

Your [Product] trial ended today.

Your work is still there. You can upgrade anytime to get full access back:
→ [Upgrade link]

If [Product] isn't right for you, I'd love to know why. Your feedback helps us improve.

Thanks for trying us out!

[Your Name]
```

## 3. Deal Acceleration Sequence

### Post-Demo (Day 0)

**Email:**
```
Subject: Great meeting + recap

Hi [Name],

Thanks for your time today! Here's a quick recap:

What we discussed:
• [Challenge 1] and how [Product] addresses it
• [Feature/benefit discussed]
• [Specific use case mentioned]

What you asked about:
• [Question 1] — [Brief answer or link]
• [Question 2] — [Brief answer or link]

Resources:
• [Relevant case study]
• [Documentation link]
• [Pricing info if discussed]

Next steps we agreed on:
• [Next step 1] by [Date]
• [Next step 2] by [Date]

Let me know if I missed anything or if questions come up.

[Your Name]
```

### Post-Demo (Day 3)

**Email:**
```
Subject: Checking in on [next step]

Hi [Name],

Following up on our call. Have you had a chance to [next step we discussed]?

Reminder of what we covered:
• [Key value prop for their situation]
• [Specific benefit they cared about]

I'm here if questions have come up. Would another quick call help move things forward?

[Your Name]
```

### Post-Demo (Day 7): Multi-Threading

**Email:**
```
Subject: Should we include your team?

Hi [Name],

As you evaluate [Product], it might help to include others who would use it or need to weigh in.

I'd be happy to:
• Do a demo for your team
• Answer questions from [IT/Finance/etc.]
• Provide materials they'd find useful

Who else should be involved? I can reach out directly or let you make the intro.

[Your Name]
```

### Proposal Follow-Up (Day 1)

**Email:**
```
Subject: Proposal delivered—questions?

Hi [Name],

I just sent over the proposal we discussed. Here's the summary:

• Solution: [Brief description]
• Investment: [Price/summary]
• Timeline: [Implementation timing]

Happy to walk through it together if that would help: [Calendar link]

Any questions so far?

[Your Name]
```

### Proposal Follow-Up (Day 5)

**Email:**
```
Subject: Any questions on the proposal?

Hi [Name],

Checking in on the proposal I sent last week.

Have you had a chance to review it? Any questions I can answer?

If it would help, I'm happy to hop on a quick call to walk through the details.

[Your Name]
```

### Proposal Follow-Up (Day 10)

**Email:**
```
Subject: What would help move this forward?

Hi [Name],

I want to make sure we're aligned on timing.

Is there anything:
• Missing from the proposal?
• That needs clarification?
• Blocking you from moving forward?

If timing isn't right, I understand—just let me know so I can follow up appropriately.

[Your Name]
```

### Negotiation Support

**Email (If Budget Concerns):**
```
Subject: Flexible options for [Company]

Hi [Name],

I heard budget might be a consideration. I wanted to share some options:

Option 1: [Lower tier/scope] at [lower price]
Option 2: [Payment plan/annual discount]
Option 3: [Pilot program]

I want to find something that works for both of us. What would be most helpful to discuss?

[Your Name]
```

## 4. Sequence Metrics to Track

| Metric | Target | What It Tells You |
|--------|--------|-------------------|
| Open rate | >50% | Subject line effectiveness |
| Reply rate | >10% | Message resonance |
| Meeting rate | >5% | Overall sequence effectiveness |
| Demo-to-proposal | >50% | Demo quality |
| Proposal-to-close | >30% | Deal qualification |
