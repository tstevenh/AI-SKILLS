# Dunning Email Templates

Dunning emails recover failed payments and prevent involuntary churn.

---

## Payment Failed - Day 1

```
Subject: Quick heads up about your payment

Hi [First Name],

We tried to process your payment for [Product], but it didn't 
go through. This happens sometimes—expired cards, bank hiccups, 
the usual.

Your account is still fully active. Just update your payment 
info to keep things running smoothly:

[Button: Update Payment Method →]

If you have questions, just reply.

[Your Name]
[Product] Team
```

## Dunning Sequence Overview

| Day | Email | Tone |
|-----|-------|------|
| 1 | Payment failed | Friendly, helpful |
| 3 | Second notice | Slightly urgent |
| 7 | Warning | Clear urgency |
| 14 | Final notice | Last chance |
| -- | Recovered | Celebratory |

---

## Dunning Best Practices

1. **Start friendly**: Assume honest mistake
2. **Make action easy**: One-click to update
3. **Show what's at stake**: But don't threaten
4. **Offer help**: Some customers have real issues
5. **Celebrate recovery**: Positive reinforcement
