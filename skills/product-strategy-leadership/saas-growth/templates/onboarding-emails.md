# Onboarding Email Templates

## Day 0: Welcome Email

```
Subject: Welcome to [Product]! Here's how to get started 🚀

Hi [First Name],

Welcome to [Product]! You've joined [X,000]+ [personas] who use us 
to [main benefit].

Let's get you set up in the next 5 minutes:

1️⃣ [First action] — [Link]
2️⃣ [Second action] — [Link]  
3️⃣ [Third action] — [Link]

If you hit any snags, just reply to this email. I read every one.

Here's to your success,
[Your Name]
[Title]

P.S. [Quick tip or bonus resource]
```

## Day 1: First Action Check

```
Subject: Did you [complete first value action] yet?

Hi [First Name],

Most successful [Product] users [achieve first milestone] on day one.

Have you [completed key action] yet?

✅ Yes! → Awesome! Here's your next step: [Link]
❌ Not yet? → No worries. Here's a quick guide: [Link]

Need help? Just reply—I'm here.

[Your Name]
```

## Email Sequence Overview

| Day | Email | Goal |
|-----|-------|------|
| 0 | Welcome | Orient, first action |
| 1 | First action check | Ensure activation |
| 3 | Feature highlight | Deepen usage |
| 5 | Use case inspiration | Expand value |
| 7 | Progress check | Mid-trial engagement |
| 10 | Social proof | Build confidence |
| 12 | Trial warning | Create urgency |
| 14 | Trial expired | Conversion push |
| 21 | Win-back | Re-engagement |
