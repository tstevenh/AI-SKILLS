# SaaS Benchmarks by Stage and Segment

## Introduction

Benchmarks provide critical context for evaluating your SaaS performance. Without them, you're operating blind—unable to know if your metrics are healthy, concerning, or exceptional. This comprehensive guide compiles benchmarks across all major SaaS metrics, segmented by company stage, customer type, and pricing tier.

---

## 1. Growth Metrics Benchmarks

### MRR/ARR Growth Rates

**By Stage:**

| Stage | ARR | Good Growth | Great Growth |
|-------|-----|-------------|--------------|
| Seed | $0-$1M | 15-20% MoM | >25% MoM |
| Series A | $1-5M | 8-12% MoM | >15% MoM |
| Series B | $5-20M | 5-8% MoM | >10% MoM |
| Series C+ | $20M+ | 3-5% MoM | >6% MoM |

**Annual Growth (YoY):**

| ARR | Below Average | Average | Good | Great |
|-----|---------------|---------|------|-------|
| $1M | <80% | 80-120% | 120-200% | >200% |
| $5M | <60% | 60-80% | 80-120% | >120% |
| $20M | <40% | 40-60% | 60-80% | >80% |
| $50M | <30% | 30-40% | 40-60% | >60% |
| $100M | <25% | 25-35% | 35-50% | >50% |

### T2D3 Framework

**Triple-Triple-Double-Double-Double:**
```
Year 1: $1M → Year 2: $3M (3x)
Year 2: $3M → Year 3: $9M (3x)
Year 3: $9M → Year 4: $18M (2x)
Year 4: $18M → Year 5: $36M (2x)
Year 5: $36M → Year 6: $72M (2x)
```

This is the path to reaching $100M ARR in ~6 years (elite SaaS trajectory).

## 6. Efficiency Benchmarks

### Rule of 40

**Formula:** Growth Rate + Profit Margin = 40+

| Score | Assessment |
|-------|------------|
| <20 | Poor - needs significant improvement |
| 20-30 | Below average |
| 30-40 | Good |
| 40-60 | Great |
| >60 | Excellent |

### Burn Multiple

**Formula:** Net Burn / Net New ARR

| Multiple | Assessment |
|----------|------------|
| >3 | Poor - burning too much |
| 2-3 | Below average |
| 1.5-2 | Average |
| 1-1.5 | Good |
| <1 | Excellent - efficient growth |

### Magic Number

**Formula:** (QoQ ARR Growth) / (Previous Q S&M Spend)

| Number | Assessment |
|--------|------------|
| <0.5 | Inefficient - fix GTM |
| 0.5-0.75 | Below average |
| 0.75-1.0 | Good |
| >1.0 | Great - invest more |

## Summary: Using Benchmarks

### How to Use Benchmarks

1. **Know your segment**: SMB ≠ Enterprise
2. **Know your stage**: Seed ≠ Series C
3. **Context matters**: Industry, market, timing
4. **Trend > point-in-time**: Improvement matters
5. **Prioritize**: Can't optimize everything
