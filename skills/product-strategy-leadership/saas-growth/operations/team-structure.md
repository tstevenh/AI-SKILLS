# SaaS Team Structure

## Introduction

How you structure your team evolves as your SaaS company grows. What works at $100K ARR doesn't work at $10M ARR. Understanding the right structure for each stage helps you hire the right people at the right time and avoid the growing pains that slow down many companies.

---

## 1. Early Stage (0-$1M ARR)

### Team Structure

**Core Team:**
- Founder/CEO (product, sales, everything)
- Co-founder/CTO (engineering)
- 2-5 Engineers

**Everyone Does Everything:**
- Founders do sales
- Engineers do support
- No specialized roles yet

### Hiring Priorities

**Order:**
1. Engineers (build product)
2. First customer-facing hire (support/success)
3. First marketer (content/demand gen)

**Don't Hire Yet:**
- Specialized sales reps
- HR/Finance
- Middle management

### Key Focus Areas

- Product-market fit
- First customers
- Learning and iteration
- Capital efficiency

---

## 2. Growth Stage ($1M-$10M ARR)

### Team Structure

```
CEO
├── Engineering (CTO)
│   ├── Product Engineers (5-15)
│   └── Infrastructure (1-3)
├── Product (VP/Head)
│   ├── Product Managers (1-3)
│   └── Design (1-3)
├── Sales (VP/Head)
│   ├── AEs (3-10)
│   └── SDRs (2-6)
├── Marketing (VP/Head)
│   ├── Demand Gen (1-2)
│   └── Content (1-2)
├── Customer Success (Head)
│   ├── CSMs (2-5)
│   └── Support (2-5)
└── Operations (Head/Ops person)
    ├── Finance
    └── HR/People
```

### Hiring Priorities

**$1M-$3M:**
1. Head of Sales (or first AEs)
2. Head of Marketing
3. Customer Success lead
4. More engineers

**$3M-$10M:**
1. VP-level leaders
2. Specialized roles
3. Middle managers
4. Support functions

### Key Focus Areas

- Repeatable sales process
- Scalable marketing channels
- Retention and expansion
- Process and documentation

## 3. Scale Stage ($10M+ ARR)

### Team Structure

```
CEO
├── CTO
│   ├── Engineering Managers
│   │   ├── Product Engineering
│   │   ├── Platform Engineering
│   │   └── Infrastructure/SRE
│   └── Security
├── CPO/VP Product
│   ├── Product Managers
│   ├── Product Design
│   ├── User Research
│   └── Product Marketing
├── CRO/VP Sales
│   ├── Sales Management
│   │   ├── SMB Team
│   │   ├── Mid-Market Team
│   │   └── Enterprise Team
│   ├── Sales Development
│   └── Sales Operations
├── CMO/VP Marketing
│   ├── Demand Generation
│   ├── Content Marketing
│   ├── Product Marketing
│   ├── Brand/Comms
│   └── Marketing Operations
├── VP Customer Success
│   ├── CS Management
│   ├── Implementation
│   ├── Support
│   └── CS Operations
├── CFO/VP Finance
│   ├── Accounting
│   ├── FP&A
│   └── Rev Ops
├── VP People/HR
│   ├── Recruiting
│   ├── People Ops
│   └── L&D
└── COO (optional)
    ├── Legal
    └── IT
```

### Hiring Priorities

**$10M-$25M:**
1. VP-level executives
2. Managers for scaling teams
3. Operations and analytics
4. Specialized individual contributors

**$25M+:**
1. C-suite executives
2. International leadership
3. Specialized functions
4. Strategic roles

## 4. Hiring Priorities by Stage

### The Hiring Stack

| Stage | Priority 1 | Priority 2 | Priority 3 |
|-------|-----------|-----------|-----------|
| 0-$500K | Engineers | First CS/Support | Founder sales |
| $500K-$1M | More engineers | First marketer | First sales hire |
| $1M-$3M | Sales team | Marketing team | CS team |
| $3M-$10M | Sales leadership | Marketing leadership | CS leadership |
| $10M+ | Executive team | Middle management | Specialists |

### Role Timing Guidelines

| Role | When to Hire |
|------|--------------|
| First engineer | Day 1 |
| First support | $100K ARR or 100 customers |
| First marketer | $300K-$500K ARR |
| First AE | $500K-$1M ARR |
| Head of Sales | $1M-$2M ARR |
| Head of Marketing | $1M-$2M ARR |
| Head of CS | $1M-$2M ARR |
| VP Sales | $3M-$5M ARR |
| VP Marketing | $3M-$5M ARR |
| VP CS | $3M-$5M ARR |
| CFO | $10M-$15M ARR |
| COO | $15M-$25M ARR |

## 5. Team Ratios

### Engineering Team

| Company Size | Engineer Ratio |
|--------------|---------------|
| <50 people | 50-60% engineers |
| 50-200 | 40-50% engineers |
| 200-500 | 35-45% engineers |
| 500+ | 30-40% engineers |

### Sales Team

| Stage | AE:SDR Ratio |
|-------|--------------|
| SMB focus | 1:0 (AEs do own prospecting) |
| Mid-market | 1:1 to 1:2 |
| Enterprise | 1:2 to 1:3 |

### Customer Success

| Model | CSM:Customer Ratio |
|-------|-------------------|
| Enterprise | 1:10-20 |
| Mid-market | 1:30-75 |
| SMB | 1:100-300 |
| Tech-touch | 1:500+ |

### Marketing

| Stage | Marketing Team Size |
|-------|-------------------|
| $1M-$3M ARR | 2-4 people |
| $3M-$10M ARR | 5-10 people |
| $10M-$25M ARR | 10-20 people |
| $25M+ ARR | 20+ people |

---

## Summary: Team Building Framework

### Principles

1. **Hire ahead of growth**: Not behind it
2. **Leaders before team**: Hire leaders who can build
3. **Generalists early, specialists later**: Flexibility matters early
4. **Culture from the start**: Define and maintain it

### Team Structure Checklist

- [ ] Org structure documented
- [ ] Hiring plan aligned to revenue
- [ ] Role definitions clear
- [ ] Compensation philosophy established
- [ ] Career paths defined
- [ ] Team ratios monitored
