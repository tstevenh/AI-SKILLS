# Product Hunt & Launch Platforms

## Introduction

Product launches can create concentrated bursts of awareness, signups, and momentum. For early-stage SaaS companies, a successful Product Hunt launch can provide thousands of users and meaningful validation. But launches require preparation—a poorly executed launch is a wasted opportunity.

---

## 1. Product Hunt Strategy

### When to Launch on Product Hunt

**Good Timing:**
- Product is polished and ready
- You have a launch-ready audience
- Team can support traffic surge
- You have compelling visuals/demo

**Bad Timing:**
- Product is buggy or incomplete
- No existing community
- Major features missing
- Can't handle traffic

### Product Hunt Success Factors

**1. Community & Upvotes**
- Existing community helps
- Day-of mobilization
- Quality over quantity
- Avoid fake upvotes (they detect)

**2. Positioning & Messaging**
- Clear, compelling tagline
- Obvious value proposition
- Visual appeal
- Easy to understand

**3. Timing**
- Launch 12:01 AM PT (optimal)
- Tuesday-Thursday best
- Avoid major holidays
- Avoid competitor launches

**4. Engagement**
- Respond to every comment
- Hunter introduction helps
- Thank supporters
- Stay active all day

### Product Hunt Launch Checklist

**Pre-Launch (2-4 weeks):**
- [ ] Hunter secured (ideally popular)
- [ ] Tagline finalized
- [ ] Description written
- [ ] 5+ images/GIFs created
- [ ] First comment drafted
- [ ] Email to supporters drafted
- [ ] Social posts scheduled
- [ ] Team aligned on day-of

**Launch Day:**
- [ ] Launch at 12:01 AM PT
- [ ] Hunter posts
- [ ] Founder comment immediately
- [ ] Notify supporter list
- [ ] Post on social media
- [ ] Monitor and respond all day
- [ ] Thank community

**Post-Launch:**
- [ ] Follow up with commenters
- [ ] Engage new signups
- [ ] Share results publicly
- [ ] Update Product Hunt page
- [ ] Plan next launch

### Product Hunt Tiers

| Position | Outcome |
|----------|---------|
| #1 Product of Day | Major visibility, 1000+ upvotes |
| Top 5 | Good visibility, 300-800 upvotes |
| Top 10 | Decent exposure, 100-300 upvotes |
| Below 10 | Limited impact, <100 upvotes |

## 2. AppSumo Considerations

### What is AppSumo?

Lifetime deal marketplace:
- Offer lifetime access at discounted price
- Access to large audience
- Strong for early traction

### AppSumo Pros & Cons

**Pros:**
- Immediate cash infusion
- Thousands of users quickly
- Marketing exposure
- Product feedback

**Cons:**
- Revenue cannibalization
- Low LTV customers
- Support burden
- Brand perception

### When to Consider AppSumo

**Good Fit:**
- Very early stage
- Need users for validation
- Can handle support volume
- Product is ready but needs traction

**Bad Fit:**
- Already have traction
- Premium positioning
- High cost to serve
- Limited support capacity

### AppSumo Deal Structure

**Typical Deal:**
- Lifetime access for $49-99
- 50% revenue share to AppSumo
- Run for 30-60 days
- Stack codes available

**Results Range:**
- Low: $10K-30K, 500-1,500 users
- Medium: $50K-100K, 2,000-5,000 users
- High: $200K+, 10,000+ users

## 3. Other Launch Platforms

### Alternatives to Product Hunt

| Platform | Focus | Reach |
|----------|-------|-------|
| Hacker News | Tech/dev | Very high |
| BetaList | Early products | Medium |
| Indie Hackers | Bootstrappers | Medium |
| Reddit (relevant subs) | Varies | High |
| Twitter/X Launch | Tech audience | Varies |

### Hacker News Launch

**Show HN Post:**
- Title: "Show HN: [Product] – [Brief description]"
- Link to product
- Comment with context

**Success Factors:**
- Genuinely interesting
- Technical angle
- Founder engagement
- Good timing

**Results:**
- Front page: Massive traffic
- Page 2-3: Good traffic
- Buried: Minimal impact

### Indie Hackers Launch

**Best Practices:**
- Share your story
- Be authentic
- Provide value
- Engage community

**What Works:**
- Building in public
- Revenue milestones
- Lessons learned
- Behind-the-scenes

## 4. Launch Playbook

### Comprehensive Launch Timeline

**Week -4: Preparation**
- Finalize product
- Create launch assets
- Build supporter list
- Draft all copy

**Week -2: Amplification Setup**
- Schedule social posts
- Prepare email sequence
- Brief team
- Test everything

**Week -1: Final Prep**
- Soft launch to friends
- Fix any issues
- Final asset review
- Confirm timing

**Launch Day:**
- Execute on all channels
- Monitor and engage
- Track metrics
- Celebrate wins

**Week +1: Follow-up**
- Nurture new signups
- Collect feedback
- Share results
- Plan next steps

### Launch Assets Needed

**Written:**
- Product name and tagline
- One-sentence description
- One-paragraph description
- Full description
- First comment/founder note
- FAQ answers

**Visual:**
- Logo (multiple sizes)
- Product screenshots (5-10)
- Product demo GIF
- Hero image
- Social share image
- Video demo (optional)

**Distribution:**
- Email to supporters
- Social media posts
- Community posts
- Direct messages
