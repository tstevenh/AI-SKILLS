# Churn Audit Checklist

## Understanding Your Churn

### Metrics
- [ ] Monthly churn rate calculated
- [ ] Gross revenue churn known
- [ ] Net revenue retention calculated
- [ ] Logo churn vs revenue churn split understood
- [ ] Voluntary vs involuntary churn split known

### Benchmarking
- [ ] Churn compared to industry benchmarks
- [ ] Churn by segment analyzed
- [ ] Churn by acquisition channel analyzed
- [ ] Churn by plan/tier analyzed

---

## Churn Analysis

### Cohort Analysis
- [ ] Churn by signup cohort tracked
- [ ] Retention curves built
- [ ] Cohort improvements visible
- [ ] Problem cohorts identified

### Reason Analysis
- [ ] Cancellation survey implemented
- [ ] Exit interviews conducted (high value)
- [ ] Top churn reasons documented
- [ ] Churn reasons quantified

### Behavioral Analysis
- [ ] Pre-churn behavior patterns identified
- [ ] Feature usage correlated with churn
- [ ] Engagement metrics analyzed
- [ ] Leading indicators found

---

## Churn Prevention

### Early Warning
- [ ] Customer health score built
- [ ] At-risk accounts identified
- [ ] Alerts for declining engagement
- [ ] Proactive outreach triggers

### Voluntary Churn
- [ ] Product gaps addressed
- [ ] Customer success playbooks
- [ ] Win-back campaigns
- [ ] Save offers in cancellation flow

### Involuntary Churn
- [ ] Card expiration alerts
- [ ] Dunning email sequence
- [ ] Multiple retry attempts
- [ ] Payment method update ease

---

## Cancellation Flow

### Design
- [ ] Cancellation survey (reasons)
- [ ] Save offers presented
- [ ] Downgrade options shown
- [ ] Pause option available
- [ ] Easy to complete (don't trap)

### Data Collection
- [ ] Reason tracked in CRM
- [ ] Feedback routed to product
- [ ] Win-back list maintained
- [ ] Analysis conducted monthly

---

## Recovery

### Win-Back
- [ ] Win-back email sequence
- [ ] Re-engagement campaigns
- [ ] Return offers
- [ ] New feature announcements to churned

### Reactivation
- [ ] Reactivation tracking
- [ ] Success stories of returns
- [ ] Door left open in offboarding

---

## Continuous Improvement

### Review Cadence
- [ ] Weekly churn metrics review
- [ ] Monthly deep dive
- [ ] Quarterly strategic review
- [ ] Annual churn reduction goals

### Action Items
- [ ] Top churn reasons have owners
- [ ] Product roadmap addresses churn causes
- [ ] CS team aligned on interventions
- [ ] Progress tracked

---

## Key Questions to Answer

1. What is our churn rate (gross and net)?
2. Who is churning (segments, cohorts)?
3. Why are they churning (reasons)?
4. When do they churn (timing patterns)?
5. Can we predict churn (leading indicators)?
6. How can we prevent churn (actions)?
7. Can we recover churned customers?

---

*End of Checklists*
