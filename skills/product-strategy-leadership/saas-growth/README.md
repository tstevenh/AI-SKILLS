# SaaS Growth Playbook

The definitive guide to building, growing, and scaling SaaS businesses.

## What It Does

This skill provides comprehensive SaaS growth knowledge covering product-led growth, pricing strategy, acquisition channels, retention optimization, and expansion revenue. Scale from $0 to $10M+ ARR.

## Features

- SaaS business models and unit economics
- Product-led growth (PLG) fundamentals
- Free trial and freemium optimization
- Pricing strategy and packaging
- Acquisition channel strategies
- Onboarding and activation frameworks
- Churn reduction and retention
- Expansion revenue and NRR optimization
- Case studies from Slack, Notion, Calendly, Loom

## Installation

1. Copy the `saas-growth/` folder into your OpenClaw skills directory
2. Restart OpenClaw

## Usage

Use this skill when:
- Planning SaaS pricing strategy
- Optimizing trial-to-paid conversion
- Reducing churn
- Designing onboarding flows
- Building acquisition channels
- Scaling operations
- Analyzing SaaS metrics

## Requirements

- Analytics platform (Mixpanel, Amplitude, or similar)
- Subscription management system
- Customer communication tools
- Cohort analysis capabilities
- Access to SaaS metrics and benchmarks
